## Why this is its own R-ID

R18a-GNT is structurally a Tier 2 formula-integrity rule (parallel to R6 fixed-phrases, R18 vocative integrity). It is given its own R-ID rather than being folded into R6 because the triad variants are not "phrases" in the R6 sense but a referring-expression family — a typed entity (YHWH-as-Patriarchs'-God) with a closed list of attested surface realizations.
