# R18 — Audit Trail

Sweep history for canon §3.9 (R18 Vocative Rule). The operational rule lives in the canon entry; this document carries the sweep records and corpus-impact evidence.

## Corpus sweep

- **2026-04, vocative-merge applier sweep:** 125 vocative merges landed across 21 books. The sweep targeted instances where a vocative followed a 2p pronoun or 2p finite verb within the same clause-span (subject-appositive or object-appositive patterns).
- The applier `scripts/archive/apply_vocative_rule.py` (archived 2026-05-16) handled the bulk pass; drift detection ongoing via `validators/colometry/check_r18_vocative.py`.

## Codification provenance

The "verb person is irrelevant" clarification was added after corpus survey found ~5 false-split candidates where the validator was incorrectly requiring 2p verb morphology rather than 2p pronoun presence (Luke 9:61 was the canonical case that surfaced the gap).

The repeated-vocative carve-out (`Κύριε κύριε`) was codified following Matt 7:21–22 and Luke 6:46 review — both attempted-split-then-merged after editorial review; codified as a structural carve-out rather than a per-case editorial call.
