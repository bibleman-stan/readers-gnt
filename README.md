# readers-nt

A colometric reading edition of the Greek New Testament.

The text of the SBLGNT is reformatted into **sense-lines (cola)** based on Greek grammatical structure, recovering the oral phrasing that ancient authors composed for. Each line is a natural breath unit designed for read-aloud delivery and comprehension.

## Why

Modern Bible formatting (verse numbers, paragraph breaks, punctuation) was added centuries after composition. These conventions obscure the original compositional structure. By breaking the text at its natural grammatical joints — clause boundaries, participial phrases, parallel structures — we make the author's own rhetoric visible.

## Status

Early development. Test chapters: Mark 4, Acts 17.

## Base Text

SBL Greek New Testament (SBLGNT), freely available for research use.
