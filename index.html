The text as it was meant to be heard.

Ancient authors composed for the ear, not the eye. Modern Bible formatting — verse numbers, paragraph breaks, punctuation — was added centuries after composition and obscures the original structure. This edition formats the Greek New Testament into sense-lines based on grammatical structure, recovering the breath units and thought patterns that the original audiences heard.
