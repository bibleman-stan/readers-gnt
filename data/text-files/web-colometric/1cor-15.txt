15:1
I make known now to you
brothers
the gospel
that I proclaimed to you
which also you received
in which also you stand

15:2
by which also you are being saved
to the word I proclaimed to you if you hold fast
unless if not in vain you have believed

15:3
I delivered for to you in the foremost
what also I received
that Christ died for the sins of us according to the Scriptures

15:4
and that He was buried
and that He was raised the day the third according to the Scriptures

15:5
and that He appeared to Cephas
then to the Twelve

15:6
Thereafter He appeared to more than five hundred brothers at once
of whom the greater part remain until now
some however have fallen asleep

15:7
Then He appeared to James
then to the apostles all

15:8
Last then of all as the untimely birth He appeared also to me

15:9
I for am the least of the apostles
who not am fit to be called an apostle
because I persecuted the church of God

15:10
By the grace however of God I am what I am
and the grace of him toward me not void has been
Rather more abundantly than them all I toiled
not I however but the grace of God that with me

15:11
Whether therefore I or they thus we preach
and thus you believed

15:12
If now Christ is preached that out from the dead He has been raised
how say among you some that a resurrection of the dead not there is

15:13
If however a resurrection of the dead not there is
neither Christ has been raised

15:14
if then Christ not has been raised
is void then the preaching of us
void also the faith of you

15:15
We are found then also false witnesses of God
because we have witnessed concerning God
that He raised up Christ
whom not He has raised
if then the dead not are raised

15:16
If for the dead not are raised
neither Christ has been raised

15:17
if then Christ not has been raised
futile the faith of you
still you are in the sins of you

15:18
Then also those having fallen asleep in Christ have perished

15:19
If in the life this in Christ having hope we are only
more to be pitied than all men we are

15:20
Now however Christ has been raised out from the dead firstfruit of those having fallen asleep

15:21
Since for by a man came death also by a man resurrection of the dead

15:22
For as indeed in Adam all die
so also in Christ all will be made alive

15:23
Each however in the own order the firstfruit Christ
then those of Christ
at the coming of him

15:24
then the end
when hands over the kingdom to the God and Father
when He shall have annulled all dominion and all authority and power

15:25
It behooves for Him to reign
until that He shall have put all the enemies under the feet of him

15:26
The last enemy to be abolished is death

15:27
All things for He has put in subjection under the feet of him
When however it may be said
that all things have been put in subjection
it is evident that is excepted the One having put in subjection to him all things

15:28
When now shall have been put in subjection to him all things
then Himself the Son will be put in subjection to the One having put in subjection to him all things
so that may be God all in all

15:29
Otherwise what will they do who are baptized for the dead
If at all the dead not are raised why
also are they baptized for them

15:30
Why also we are in danger every hour

15:31
Every day I die as surely as the in you boasting
which I have in Christ Jesus the Lord of us

15:32
If according to man I fought wild beasts in Ephesus
what to me the profit
If the dead not are raised
Let us eat and let us drink
tomorrow for we die

15:33
Not be misled
Do corrupt morals good companionships bad

15:34
Sober up righteously and not sin
ignorance for of God some have
to the shame of you I speak

15:35
But will say someone
How are raised the dead
With what then body do they come

15:36
Fool
you What you sow
not does come to life if not it dies

15:37
And what you sow
not the body that will be you sow but a bare grain
if it may be of wheat or of some of the rest

15:38
But God gives it a body
as He has willed and to each of the seeds its own body

15:39
Not all flesh is the same flesh but one indeed of men
another now flesh of beasts
another now flesh of birds
another now of fish

15:40
And bodies there are heavenly and bodies earthly
But one truly is the of the heavenly glory
another now that of the earthly

15:41
One is the glory of the sun
and another the glory of the moon
and another the glory of the stars
star for from star differs in glory

15:42
So also is the resurrection of the dead
It is sown in decay it is raised in immortality

15:43
It is sown in dishonor it is raised in glory
It is sown in weakness it is raised in power

15:44
It is sown a body natural it is raised a body spiritual If there is a body natural there is also spiritual

15:45
So also it has been written
Became the first man Adam into a soul living
the last Adam into a spirit life-giving

15:46
However not first was the spiritual but the natural then the spiritual

15:47
The first man was from the earth made of dust
the second man from heaven

15:48
As the one made of dust
so also are those of the earth
and as the heavenly one
so also those of heaven

15:49
And as we have born the image of the earthly
bear also the image of the heavenly

15:50
This now I say
brothers
that flesh and blood the kingdom of God to inherit
not is able nor the decay the immortality does inherit

15:51
Behold a mystery to you I tell
All not we will sleep
all however we will be changed

15:52
in an instant in the twinkling of an eye at the last trumpet The trumpet will sound
for and the dead will be raised imperishable
and we will be changed

15:53
It behooves for the perishable this to put on imperishable
and the mortal this to put on immortality

15:54
When now the perishable this shall have put on imperishable
and the mortal this shall have put on immortality then will come to pass the word having been written
Has been swallowed up death in victory

15:55
Where of you
O death
the victory
Where of you
O death
the sting

15:56
And the sting of death is sin and the power of sin the law

15:57
to however God be thanks the One giving us the victory through the Lord of us Jesus Christ

15:58
Therefore
brothers of me beloved
steadfast be immovable
abounding in the work of the Lord always
knowing that the toil of you not is in vain in the Lord
