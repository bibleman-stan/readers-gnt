7:1
Or are you ignorant
brothers
to those knowing for law I speak that the law rules over the man
for as long as the time he is alive

7:2
The for married woman to the living husband is bound by law
if however should die the husband she is cleared from the law of the husband

7:3
Then therefore being alive the husband
an adulteress she will be called if she is to man another
if however should die the husband free she is
from the law so as for not to be her an adulteress having been to man another

7:4
Likewise
brothers of me
also you have been put to death to the law through the body of Christ
for to belong you to another to the One out from the dead having been raised
so that we should bear fruit to God

7:5
While for we were in the flesh
the passions of sins that were through the law were at work in the members of us
to the bringing forth of fruit to death

7:6
Now however we have been released from the law having died to that which we were bound
in order for to serve us in newness of the Spirit and not in oldness of the letter

7:7
What then shall we say
Is the law sin
Never may it be
But sin not I have known if not by law
then for covetousness not I had been conscious of if not the law had said
Not you shall covet

7:8
An occasion however having taken
sin by the commandment it produced in me all covetousness
apart from for the Law sin is dead

7:9
I however was alive apart from law once
having come however the commandment
the sin revived

7:10
I then died
And proved to be me the commandment that was to life this to death

7:11
The for sin an occasion having taken
by the commandment deceived me
and by it put me to death

7:12
So indeed the law is holy
and the commandment is holy and righteous and good

7:13
That which then is good to me has become death
Never may it be
But sin in order that it might be shown to be sin
through that which is good to me is working out death
so that might become beyond excess sinful sin through the commandment

7:14
We know for that the Law spiritual is
I however fleshly am having been sold under sin

7:15
What for I do
not I understand not
for what I want this I do
but what I hate this I do

7:16
If now that which not I do want this I do
I consent to the law that it is good

7:17
In that case now no longer I am doing it
but the dwells in me sin

7:18
I know for that nothing there dwells in me that is in the flesh of me
good
for to will is present with me
but to do the good not

7:19
Not for that I desire I do good but that not I do want
evil this I practice

7:20
If now what not I do want this do
it is no longer I who do it
but dwelling in me sin

7:21
I find so the principle that desiring me to do good
that me evil is present with

7:22
I delight for in the law of God according to the inward man

7:23
I see however another law in the members of me warring against the law of the mind of me
and making captive me to the law of sin
being in the members of me

7:24
O wretched I am man
Who me will deliver out of the body of death this

7:25
Thanks be to God through Jesus Christ the Lord of us
Then so myself I with the indeed mind serve law God’s
but with the flesh the law of sin
