1:1
Simon Peter a servant and apostle of Jesus Christ
To those equally precious with ours having obtained a faith
through the righteousness of the God of us and Savior Jesus Christ

1:2
Grace to you and peace be multiplied
in the knowledge of God and of Jesus the Lord of us

1:3
Accordingly all things to us the divine power of him
toward life and godliness has given
through the knowledge of the One having called us by His own glory and excellence

1:4
through which the precious and magnificent to us promises He has given
so that through these you might become of the divine partakers nature
having escaped the in the world in desire decay

1:5
And very reason for this now earnestness all having brought in
supplement into the faith of you virtue
into and virtue knowledge

1:6
into then knowledge self-control
into then self-control endurance
into then endurance godliness

1:7
in then godliness brotherly affection
in then brotherly affection love

1:8
These things for in you being and abounding
neither idle nor unfruitful make you
as to the of the Lord of us Jesus Christ knowledge

1:9
In whomever for not are present these things
blind he is being short sighted
forgetfulness having received of the purification the former of him sins

1:10
Therefore rather
brothers
be diligent sure your calling and election to make
these things for practicing never not shall you stumble at any time

1:11
In this way for richly will be supplied to you the entrance
into the eternal kingdom of the Lord of us and Savior Jesus Christ

1:12
Therefore I will be ready always you to remind concerning these things
though knowing them and having been strengthened in the being present in you truth

1:13
Right now I esteem it
as long as I am in this tabernacle
to stir up you by putting you in remembrance

1:14
knowing that impending is the putting off of the tabernacle of me
as also the Lord of us Jesus Christ has made clear to me

1:15
I will be diligent now also at every time to have for you
after my departure these things a lasting remembrance to make

1:16
Not for cleverly devised fables having followed out
we have made known to you the of the Lord of us Jesus Christ power and coming
but eyewitnesses having been of His majesty

1:17
Having received for from God the Father honor and glory
a voice was brought to him such as follows by the Majestic Glory
The Son of me beloved of me this is
in whom I have found delight

1:18
And this voice we heard from heaven having been brought
with him being in the holy mountain

1:19
And we have more certain the prophetic word
to which well you do taking heed
as to a lamp shining in a dark place
until this day shall have dawned
and the morning star shall have arisen in the hearts of you

1:20
this first knowing
that any prophecy of Scripture of its own interpretation not is

1:21
Not for by the will of man was brought prophecy at any time
but by the Holy Spirit being carried spoke from God men
