9:1
And He was saying to them
Truly
I say to you
that there are some of those here standing
who no not shall taste of death
until they see the kingdom of God having come with power

9:2
And after days six takes along Jesus Peter and James and John
and brings up them into a mountain high apart themselves alone
And He was transfigured before them

9:3
and the garments of him became shining white exceedingly
such as a launderer on the earth not is able thus to whiten

9:4
And appeared to them Elijah with Moses
and they were talking with Jesus

9:5
And answering Peter says to Jesus
Rabbi good it is
for us here to be
And let us make three tabernacles
for You one and for Moses one and Elijah one

9:6
Not for he knew what he should say
terrified for they were

9:7
And there came a cloud
overshadowing them
and there came a voice out of the cloud
This is the Son of me the beloved
listen you to him

9:8
And suddenly having looked around no longer no one they saw
except Jesus alone with them

9:9
And descending of them from the mountain
He instructed them that to no one
what they had seen they should tell if not until the Son of Man out from the dead had risen

9:10
And that saying they kept
among themselves questioning what it is out from the dead to rise

9:11
And they were asking Him saying
Say the scribes that Elijah it behooves to come first

9:12
And He was saying to them
Elijah indeed having come first restores all things
and how has it been written of the Son of Man that many things He should suffer
and be set at naught

9:13
But I say to you that also Elijah has come and they did to him
whatever they desired as it has been written of him

9:14
And having come to the disciples they saw a crowd great around them
and scribes arguing with them

9:15
And immediately all the crowd having seen Him were greatly amazed
and running to him were greeting Him

9:16
And He asked them
What are you disputing with them

9:17
And answered Him one out of the crowd
Teacher I brought the son of me to You
having a spirit mute

9:18
and whenever if him it seizes
it throws down him and he foams
and gnashes his teeth
and is withering away
And I spoke to the disciples of You that it they might cast out
and not they had power

9:19
And answering him He says
O generation unbelieving Until when with you will I be
Until when will I bear with you
Bring him to me

9:20
And they brought him to him
And having seen Him the spirit immediately threw into convulsions him
and having fallen upon the ground he began rolling around foaming

9:21
And He asked the father of him
How long a time is it that this has been with him
And he said From childhood

9:22
And often both into fire him it casts
and into waters
that it might destroy him
But if anything You are able to do
help us having compassion on us

9:23
And Jesus said to him
If You are able All things are possible to the one believing

9:24
Immediately having cried out the father of the child was saying
I believe
help of me the unbelief

9:25
Having seen now Jesus that was running together a crowd
He rebuked the unclean spirit saying to it
Mute and deaf spirit I command you come out of him
and no more may you enter into him

9:26
And having cried out and much having thrown him into convulsions it came out
and he became as if dead
in order for many to say that he was dead

9:27
And Jesus having taken by the hand him raised up him and he arose

9:28
And having entered He into a house
the disciples of him in private were asking Him
Why we not were able to cast out it

9:29
And He said to them
This kind by nothing is able to go out
if not by prayer

9:30
From there having gone forth they were passing through Galilee
and not did He want that anyone should know it

9:31
He was teaching for the disciples of him
and He was saying to them The Son of Man is delivered into the hands of men
and they will kill Him
and having been killed on the third day He will arise

9:32
And they did not understand the saying
and they were afraid Him to ask

9:33
And they came to Capernaum
And in the house having been He was asking them
What on the way were you discussing

9:34
And they were silent
with one another for they had been discussing along the road which was greatest

9:35
And having sat down He called the Twelve
and He says to them
If anyone desires first to be he will be of all last and of all servant

9:36
And having taken a child
He set it in midst of them
and having taken in His arms it He said to them

9:37
Whoever one of such little children shall receive in the name of me
Me receives
and whoever Me shall receive not Me receives
but the One having sent Me

Answered Him John
Teacher we saw someone in the name of You casting out demons
and we were forbidding him because not he was following us

9:39
And Jesus said
Not forbid him
No one for there is
who will do a work of power in the name of me
and will be able readily to speak evil of me

9:40
Whoever for not is against us
for us is

9:41
Whoever for might give to drink you a cup of water in name
because Christ’s you are
truly I say to you
that no not shall he lose the reward of him

better for it is him rather if is put a millstone heavy around the neck of him

better for it is you

And if the foot of you should cause to stumble you
cut off it
better for it is you
to enter into life lame
than the two feet having to be cast into hell

9:47
And if the eye of you should cause to stumble you
cast out it
better for you it is
with one eye to enter into the kingdom of God
than two eyes having to be cast into hell

where the worm of them not dies
and the fire not is quenched

Good is the salt
