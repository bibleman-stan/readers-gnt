13:1
And going forth of him out of the temple
says to him one of the disciples of him
Teacher behold what stones and what buildings

13:2
And Jesus said to him
See you these great buildings
No not shall be left here stone upon stone
which no not shall be thrown down

13:3
And sitting of him upon the Mount of Olives opposite the temple
asked Him in private Peter and James and John and Andrew

13:4
Tell us when these things will be And what will be the sign
when are going these things to be accomplished all

13:5
And Jesus began to say to them
Take heed
lest anyone you mislead

13:6
Many will come in the name of me
saying I am He
and many they will mislead

13:7
When then you shall hear of wars and rumors of wars
not be disturbed
it must come to pass
but not yet is the end

13:8
Will rise up for nation against nation
and kingdom against kingdom There will be earthquakes throughout places there will be famines
The beginning of birth pains are these

13:9
Take heed however you to yourselves
they will betray you to courts
and in synagogues you will be beaten
and before governors and kings you will stand because of me for a testimony to them

13:10
And to all the nations first it behooves to proclaim the gospel

13:11
And when they might lead away you delivering you up not be anxious beforehand what you should say
but whatever if might be given to you in that hour
that speak
not for are you those speaking
but the Holy Spirit

13:12
And will deliver up brother brother to death
and father child and will rise up children against parents
and will put to death them

13:13
And you will be hated by all on account of the name of me
the one however having endured to the end he will be saved

13:14
When then you see the abomination of the desolation standing
where not it should the one reading let him understand
then those in Judea let them flee to the mountains

13:15
the one upon the housetop not let him come down
nor go in
anything to take out of the house of him

13:16
and the one in the field not let him return to the things behind to take the clothing of him

13:17
Woe
then to those in womb having and to the ones nursing infants in those days

13:18
Pray then that it not might be in winter

13:19
will be in for the days those tribulation
such as never has been the like from the beginning of creation
which created God
until now and never not shall be

13:20
And if not had shortened the Lord the days
no there would have been saved any flesh
but on account of the elect whom He chose
He has shortened the days

13:21
And then if anyone to you says
Behold here is the Christ
Behold there not you shall believe it

13:22
There will arise for false Christs and false prophets
and perform signs and wonders so as to deceive if possible the elect

13:23
You however take heed
I have foretold to you all things

13:24
But in those days after the tribulation those The sun will be darkened
and the moon not will give the light of it

13:25
and the stars will be out of the heaven falling
and the powers that are in the heavens will be shaken

13:26
And then will they see the Son of Man coming
in the clouds with power great and glory

13:27
And then He will send the angels
and will gather together the elect from the four
winds from the end of earth to the end of heaven

13:28
Of now the fig tree learn the parable
When already the branch of it tender has become
and it puts forth the leaves
you know that near the summer is

13:29
So also you
when you see these things coming to pass know that near He is at the doors

13:30
Truly I say to you
that no not will have passed away the generation this until
that these things all shall have taken place

13:31
The heaven and the earth will pass away
but the words of me in no way * will pass away

13:32
Concerning now the day that or the hour no one knows
not even the angels in heaven nor the Son if not the Father

13:33
Take heed
watch
not you know for when the time is

13:34
It is like a man going on a journey having left the house of him
and having given the servants of him authority
to each one the work of him And the doorkeeper he commanded that he should keep watch

13:35
Watch
therefore not you know for when the master of the house comes
or at evening or at midnight or when the rooster crows or morning

13:36
lest having come suddenly he should find you sleeping

13:37
What now to you I say
to all I say Watch
