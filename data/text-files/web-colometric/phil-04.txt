4:1
Therefore,
My brothers beloved and longed for,
The joy and my crown,
In this way stand firm in the Lord,
Beloved.

4:2
Euodia I exhort and Syntyche I exhort the same to be of mind in the Lord.

4:3
Yes I ask also you,
True yokefellow,
Help these women,
Who in the gospel labored together with me
With also Clement and the rest of the fellow my workers,
Whose names are in the book of life.

Again I will say rejoice.

The Lord is near;

But in everything by prayer and supplication
With thanksgiving the requests of you let be made known to God;

And the minds of you in Christ Jesus.
