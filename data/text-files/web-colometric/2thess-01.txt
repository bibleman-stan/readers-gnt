1:1
Paul and Silvanus and Timothy
To the church of the Thessalonians in our God Father and the Lord Jesus Christ;

1:2
Grace to you and peace from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

1:3
To thank we ought God always concerning you brothers,
Just as fitting it is,
Because is increasing exceedingly the faith of you
And is abounding the love of one each of all you to one another,

1:4
So as for ourselves us in you to boast in the churches of God
About the perseverance of you and faith
In all the persecutions of you and in the tribulations that you are bearing,

1:5
This is a plain token of the righteous judgment of God,
Unto to be accounted worthy you of the kingdom of God,
For which also you suffer,

1:6
For indeed righteous it is with God to repay those oppressing you with affliction

1:7
And to you being oppressed repose with us
At the revelation of the Lord Jesus from heaven with the angels mighty his

1:8
In flaming fire inflicting vengeance
On those not knowing God
And on those not obeying the gospel of our Lord Jesus,

1:9
Who the penalty will suffer of destruction eternal away from the presence of the Lord
And from the glory of the power his,

1:10
When He shall come to be glorified in the saints his
And to be marveled at among all those having believed,
Because was believed our testimony to you,
In the day that.

1:11
For which also we pray always for you,
That you He may count worthy of the calling of our God
And He may fulfill every good pleasure of goodness and work of faith with power,

1:12
So that may be glorified the name of our Lord Jesus in you,
And you in Him according to the grace of our God and the Lord Jesus Christ.
