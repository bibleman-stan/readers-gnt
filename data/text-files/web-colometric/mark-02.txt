2:1
And He having entered again into Capernaum after some days it was heard that in the house He is

2:2
And were gathered together many
so that no more to have space not even at the door
and He was speaking to them the word

2:3
And they come bringing to him a paralytic
being carried by four

2:4
And not being able to come near to him
on account of the crowd they removed the roof
where He was
and having broken up it they let down the pallet
on which the paralytic was lying

2:5
And having seen Jesus the faith of them
He says to the paralytic
Son are forgiven of you the sins

2:6
Were now some of the scribes there
sitting and reasoning in the hearts of them

2:7
Why this man thus does speak
He blasphemes
Who is able to forgive sins
if not alone God

2:8
And immediately having known Jesus in the spirit of him that thus they are reasoning within themselves
He says to them
Why these things reason you in the hearts of you

2:9
Which is easier to say to the paralytic
Are forgiven of you the sins
or to say
Arise
and take up the mat of you and walk

2:10
That however you might know that authority has the Son of Man
on the earth to forgive sins
He says to the paralytic

2:11
To you I say arise take up the mat of you
and go to the home of you

2:12
And he arose
and immediately having taken up the mat
he went forth before all
so as for to be amazed all
and to glorify God
saying Thus never did we see

2:13
And He went forth again beside the sea
and all the crowd was coming to him
and He taught them

2:14
And passing on He saw Levi the son of Alphaeus sitting at the tax booth and He says to him
Follow Me
And having arisen he followed Him

2:15
And it came to pass reclining of him in the house of him
that many tax collectors and sinners were reclining with Jesus and the disciples of him
there were
for many and they were following Him

2:16
And the scribes of the Pharisees
having seen Him eating with the sinners and tax collectors
were saying to the disciples of him Why with the tax collectors and sinners does He eat

2:17
And having heard Jesus says to them No need have those being strong
of a physician but those sick being
Not I came to call the righteous
but sinners

2:18
And were the disciples of John and the Pharisees fasting
And they come
and say to him
Because of why
the disciples of John and the disciples of the Pharisees fast
but Your disciples not fast

2:19
And said to them Jesus Not are able the sons of the bridechamber
in which the bridegroom with them is to fast
As long as time they have the bridegroom
with them
not they are able to fast

2:20
Will come however the days
when will have been taken away from them the bridegroom
and then they will fast in those the days

2:21
No one a patch of cloth unshrunk sews on clothing old
if
now not tears away the patch from it new from the old
And worse a tear takes place

2:22
And no one puts wine new into wineskins old
if
now not will burst the wine the wineskins
and the wine will be destroyed
and the wineskins Instead wine new into wineskins new

2:23
And it came to pass He on the Sabbaths is passing through the grainfields
and the disciples of him began their way to make
plucking the heads of grain

2:24
And the Pharisees were saying to him
Behold why do they on the Sabbaths that which not is lawful

2:25
And He said to them
Never did you read what did David when need he had
and hungered he and those with him

2:26
How he entered into the house of God in the days of Abiathar the high priest
and the loaves of the presentation ate
which not it is lawful to eat
if not for the priests
And he gave even to those with him being

2:27
And He said to them
The Sabbath on account of the man was made
and not the man on account of the Sabbath

2:28
So then Lord is the Son of Man also even of the Sabbath
