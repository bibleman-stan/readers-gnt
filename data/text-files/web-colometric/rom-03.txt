What then is the superiority of the Jew
Or what is the benefit of the circumcision

3:2
Much in every way
Chiefly indeed for that they were entrusted with the oracles of God

What for if disbelieved some
Not the unbelief of them the faithfulness of God will nullify

3:4
Never may it be
Let be however God true
every now man a liar
as it has been written
That You may be justified in the words of You
and will prevail in being judged Your

If however the unrighteousness of us God’s righteousness shows
what shall we say
Is unrighteous God inflicting the wrath
According to man I speak

Never may it be
Otherwise how will judge God the world

3:7
If however the truth of God in my lie abounded to the glory of him
why still also I as a sinner am judged

3:8
And is it not as we are slanderously charged
and as affirm some that us to say Let us do things evil
that may come the good things

What then Are we better
Not at all We have already charged for Jews both and Greeks all under sin to be

none there is understanding none there is seeking after God

none there is is practicing good
not there is so much as one

A grave having been opened the throat of them is with the tongues of them they keep practicing deceit
the venom of vipers is under the lips of them

3:14
of whom the mouth of cursing and of bitterness is full

3:15
swift are the feet of them to shed blood

3:16
ruin and misery are in the paths of them

3:17
and the way of peace not they have known

3:18
Not there is fear of God before the eyes of them

3:19
We know now that whatever the law says to those under the law it speaks so that every mouth may be stopped
and under judgment may be all the world to God

Therefore by works of the Law not will be justified any flesh before him
through for the Law is knowledge of sin

3:21
Now however apart from law the righteousness of God has been revealed
being borne witness to by the Law and the Prophets

3:22
the righteousness now of God through faith from Jesus Christ toward all those believing
Not for there is distinction

All for have sinned
and fall short of the glory of God

3:24
being justified freely by the of him grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus

3:25
whom set forth as God a propitiation through faith in His blood
for a showing forth of the righteousness of him because of the forbearance of the having taken place beforehand sins

3:26
in the forbearance of God for of the showing forth the righteousness of him
in the present time for to be Him just
and justifying the one of faith of Jesus

Where then is the boasting It has been excluded

We reckon therefore to be justified by faith a man apart from works of the Law

Or of Jews is He the God only
not also of Gentiles
Yes also of Gentiles

since indeed One God is
