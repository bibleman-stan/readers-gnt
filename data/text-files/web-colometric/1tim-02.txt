2:1
I exhort therefore first of all to be made
Entreaties prayers intercessions and thanksgivings on behalf of all men,

2:2
For kings and all those in authority being,
So that a tranquil and quiet life we may lead in all godliness and dignity.

2:3
This is good and acceptable our before Savior God,

2:4
Who all men desires to be saved and to the knowledge of the truth to come.

2:5
For one God there is,
One then mediator between God and men the man Christ Jesus,

2:6
Who given Himself as a ransom for all,
The testimony in proper times their own;

2:7
In regard to which was appointed I a herald and
The truth I am speaking not a teacher of the Gentiles in faith and truth.

2:8
I desire therefore to pray the men in every place,
Lifting up holy hands apart from anger and dissension.

2:9
Not with braided hair or gold or pearls or clothing costly,
[...]
,

2:10
But with what is becoming to women
Professing the fear of God,
Through works good.

2:11
A woman in quietness let learn in all submissiveness;

2:12
To teach however a woman not I do permit nor to use authority over a man,
But to be in quietness.

2:13
For adam first was formed,
Then Eve;

2:14
And Adam not was deceived,
But the woman having been deceived into transgression has come.

2:15
She will be saved however through childbearing,
If they abide in faith and love and holiness with self-restraint.
