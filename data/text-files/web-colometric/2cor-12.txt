12:1
To boast it behooves me
not is it profitable indeed I will go on however to visions and revelations of the Lord

12:2
I know a man in Christ ago years fourteen
whether in the body not I know or out of the body not I know
God knows having been caught up such a man to the third heaven

12:3
And I know such a man
whether in the body or out of the body not I know God knows

12:4
that he was caught up into Paradise
and he heard inexpressible words
not being permitted to man to speak

12:5
Concerning such a man I will boast
concerning however myself not I will boast if not in the weaknesses

12:6
If for I should desire to boast
not I will be a fool
the truth for I will be speaking I refrain
however lest anyone to me should credit more than what he sees in me
or hears anything of me

12:7
and the surpassingness of the revelations Therefore
that not I should become conceited
was given to me a thorn in my flesh
a messenger of Satan that me he might buffet
so that not I should become conceited

12:8
For this three times the Lord I begged that it might depart from me

12:9
And He said to me
Suffices you the grace of me
the for power in weakness is perfected
Most gladly therefore rather will I boast
in the weaknesses my
so that may rest upon me
the power of Christ

12:10
Therefore I take pleasure in weaknesses in insults in hardships
in persecutions and difficulties for Christ
when for I might be weak
then strong I am

12:11
I have become a fool
you me compelled
I for ought by you to have been commended
In no way
for was I inferior to those most eminent apostles though even nothing I am

12:12
The indeed signs of the apostle were performed among you
in all perseverance in signs both and wonders and miracles

12:13
In what for is it that you were inferior beyond the rest of the churches
if not that myself I not did burden you
Forgive me the wrong this

12:14
Behold third time this ready I am to come to you
and not I will burden you
not for I seek what is yours
but you Not for ought the children for the parents to treasure up
but the parents for the children

12:15
I now most gladly will spend and will be utterly spent for the souls of you
If more abundantly you love
less I am loved

12:16
Be it so however I not did burden you
but being crafty by trickery you I caught

12:17
Not any of whom I have sent to you
by him did I exploit you

12:18
I urged Titus to go and sent with him the brother
Not did exploit you Titus
Not in the same spirit did we walk
Not in the same steps

12:19
All along have you been thinking that to you we have been making a defense
Before God in Christ we speak
but all things
beloved
for your edification

12:20
I fear for lest perhaps having come
not such as I wish I may find you
and I might be found by you
such as not you do wish lest perhaps quarreling jealousy anger contentions
slander gossip conceit disorder

12:21
lest again having come of me
should humble me the God of me as to you
and I should mourn over many of those having before sinned
and not having repented of the impurity and sexual immorality and sensuality that they have practiced
