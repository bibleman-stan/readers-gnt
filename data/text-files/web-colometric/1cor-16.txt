16:1
Concerning now the collection which is for the saints
as I have directed the churches of Galatia
so also you are to do

16:2
Every first of the week each of you aside him let put
treasuring up what if he may be prospered in
so that not when I might come
then collections there should be

16:3
When then I shall have arrived
whomever if you might approve with letters
these I will send to carry the bounty of you to Jerusalem

16:4
If then suitable it is for me also to go
with me they will go

16:5
I will come however to you
when Macedonia I shall have gone through
Macedonia for I am going through

16:6
With you then possibly stay
or even I will winter
so that you me may equip
wherever if I may go

16:7
Not I want for you now in passing to see
I hope indeed a time certain to remain with you if the Lord permits

16:8
I will remain however in Ephesus until Pentecost

16:9
A door for to me has opened great and productive
and are opposing many

16:10
If now comes Timothy see
that without fear he might be with you
the for work of the Lord he is doing as also I

16:11
Not anyone therefore him should despise
Equip however him
in peace so that he might come to me I am expecting for him with the brothers

16:12
Concerning now Apollos the brother greatly I exhorted him that he should go to you with the brothers
and at all not was his will that now he should come he will come however when he shall have opportunity

16:13
Watch you stand firm in the faith
act like men be strong

16:14
All things of you in love let be done

16:15
I exhort now you
brothers
you know the house of Stephanas that it is firstfruit of Achaia
and to service to the saints they have devoted themselves

16:16
that also you be subject to such as these and to everyone joining in the work and laboring

16:17
I rejoice however at the coming of Stephanas and Fortunatus and Achaicus
because your deficiency these have filled up

16:18
They refreshed for my spirit and yours
Recognize therefore such as these

16:19
Greet you the churches of Asia
Greet you in the Lord heartily Aquila
and Prisca with the at house of them church

16:20
Greet you the brothers all
Greet one another with a kiss holy

16:21
The greeting the own hand of Paul

16:22
If anyone not loves the Lord let him be accursed Maranatha

16:23
The grace of the Lord Jesus be with you

16:24
The love of me be with all you in Christ Jesus
