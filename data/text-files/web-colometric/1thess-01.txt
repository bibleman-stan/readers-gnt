1:1
Paul and Silvanus and Timothy
To the church of the Thessalonians in God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ Grace to you and peace.

1:2
We give thanks to God always concerning all of you
Mention making in our prayers unceasingly

1:3
Remembering your
Work of faith
And labor of love
And the endurance of the hope
Of our Lord Jesus Christ before God and our Father,

1:4
Knowing,
Brothers beloved by God,
The election of you,

1:5
Because our gospel
Not came to you in word only but also in power
And in the Spirit Holy and full assurance much,
Just as you know what we were among you on account of you;

1:6
And you imitators our became and of the Lord,
Having received the word in tribulation much with the joy of the Spirit Holy,

1:7
So as for to became you an example to all the believing ones
In Macedonia and in Achaia.

1:8
From you for has sounded forth the word of the Lord not only in Macedonia and Achaia,
But in every place the faith of you toward God has gone abroad,
So as for no need to have us to say anything;

1:9
They themselves for concerning us report what reception we had from you,
And how you turned to God from idols
To serve God living and true,

1:10
And to await his Son from the heavens,
Whom He raised out from the dead Jesus the one delivering us from the wrath coming.
