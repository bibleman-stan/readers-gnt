16:1
And having passed the Sabbath
Mary Magdalene and Mary the mother of James and Salome bought spices
that having come they might anoint Him

16:2
And very early on the first day of the week they come to the tomb
having arisen the sun

16:3
And they were saying among themselves
Who will roll away for us the stone from the door of the tomb

16:4
And having looked up they see that rolled away the stone
for it was large extremely

16:5
And having entered into the tomb
they saw a young man sitting on the right
clothed in a white robe
and they were greatly amazed

16:6
And he says to them
Not be amazed
Jesus you seek the Nazarene the One having been crucified
He is risen
Not He is here
Behold the place
where they laid Him

16:7
But go
say the disciples of him and to Peter that He goes before you into Galilee
there Him will you see
as He said to you

16:8
And having gone out they fled from the tomb
Had seized for them trembling and amazement
and to none nothing they spoke they were afraid
for. [And all
and that commanded those around Peter briefly told
after and afterward also himself Jesus from east
even to west sent out through them the sacred
and imperishable proclamation eternal salvation amen

16:9
[Having risen now early the first day of the week He appeared first to Mary Magdalene
from whom He had cast out seven demons

16:10
She having gone told it to those with him having been mourning and weeping

16:11
And they having heard that He is alive and has been seen by her disbelieved

16:12
After now these things to two of them as they are walking He appeared in another form
going into the country

16:13
And they having gone told it to the rest
neither them did they believe

16:14
Afterward now were reclining they to the eleven He appeared
and rebuked the unbelief of them and hardness of heart
because those having seen Him arisen not they believed

16:15
And He said to them
Having gone into the world all proclaim the gospel to all the creation

16:16
The one having believed and having been baptized will be saved
the one however having disbelieved will be condemned

16:17
Signs now those having believed these will accompany
In the name of me demons they will cast out
with tongues they will speak new

16:18
serpents they will take up
and if deadly anything they drink
no not them shall it hurt
upon the sick hands they will lay
and well they will be

16:19
Indeed therefore the Lord Jesus after speaking to them
was taken up into the heaven
and sat at the right hand of God

16:20
They now having gone forth preached everywhere the Lord working with them
and the word confirming by the accompanying signs
