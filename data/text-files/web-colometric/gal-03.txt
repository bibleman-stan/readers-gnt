3:1
O foolish Galatians Who you has bewitched,
Whose before eyes Jesus Christ was publicly portrayed as having been crucified?

3:2
This only I wish to learn from you,
By works of the Law the Spirit did you receive or by hearing of faith?

3:3
So foolish are you?
Having begun in the Spirit now in the flesh are you being perfected?

3:4
So many things did you suffer in vain?
If indeed also in vain.

3:5
The one therefore supplying to you the Spirit
And working miracles among you
Is it out of works of the Law or out of hearing of faith?

3:6
So also Abraham believed God,
And it was reckoned to him as righteousness.

3:7
Know then that those of faith these sons are of Abraham.

3:8
Having foreseen then the Scripture that by faith justifies the Gentiles God
Foretold the gospel to Abraham Will be blessed in you all the nations.

3:9
So then those of faith are blessed along with the believing Abraham.

3:10
As many as for of works of the Law are
Under a curse are,
It has been written for Cursed is everyone
Who not does continue all things having been written in the book of the Law
To do them.

3:11
That now by the law no one is justified before God
Is evident because The righteous by faith will live,

3:12
And the Law not is of faith,
Rather The one having done these things will live by them.

3:13
Christ us redeemed from the curse of the Law
Having become for us a curse for it has been written;
Cursed is everyone hanging on a tree,

3:14
So that to the Gentiles the blessing of Abraham might come in Christ Jesus,
So that the promise of the Spirit we might receive through faith.

3:15
Brothers according to man I am speaking;
Even of man having been ratified a covenant no one sets aside or adds thereto.

3:16
And to Abraham were spoken the promises and to the seed of him;
Not it does say;
And to seeds as of many but as of One;
And to the seed of you,
Who is Christ.

3:17
This now I say;
The covenant having been confirmed beforehand by God
Afterward four hundred and thirty years having come
The law not does annul so as to nullify the promise.

3:18
If for by the Law is the inheritance,
It is no longer by a promise;
But to Abraham through a promise
Has granted it God.

3:19
Why then the Law?
Transgressions on account of it was added until
Should have come the seed
To whom promise has been made having been ordained through angels in the hand of a mediator;

3:20
However a mediator of one person not is,
But God one is.

3:21
The therefore Law is contrary to the promises of God?
Never may it be;
If for had been given
A law being able to impart life,
Indeed from out of the Law would have emerged righteousness.

3:22
But imprisoned the Scripture things all under sin,
So that the promise by faith from Jesus Christ might be given to those believing.

3:23
Before now came faith
Under the Law we were held in custody
Having been locked up until the being about faith to be revealed.

3:24
So that the Law our trainer has become unto Christ,
So that by faith we might be justified;

3:25
Having come now faith
No longer under a trainer we are.

3:26
For all sons of God you are through faith in Christ Jesus.

3:27
As many as for into Christ you were baptized,
Christ you have put on;

3:28
Neither there is Jew nor Greek,
Neither there is slave nor free,
Neither there is male and female;
All for you one are in Christ Jesus.

3:29
If now you are Christ’s,
Then Abraham’s seed you are,
According to the promise heirs.
