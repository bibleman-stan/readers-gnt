5:1
We know for that if earthly of us house the tent should be destroyed
a building from God we have a house not made with hands eternal in the heavens

5:2
And indeed in this we groan
the dwelling of us which is from heaven to be clothed with longing

5:3
If indeed also having been clothed not naked we will be found

5:4
And for being in the tent we groan being burdened
because that not we do wish to be unclothed but to be clothed
that may be swallowed up the mortal by life

5:5
The One now having prepared us for very this
is God having given to us the pledge of the Spirit

5:6
being confident therefore always and knowing
that being at home in the body
we are absent from the Lord

5:7
by faith for we walk not by sight

5:8
We are confident now and are pleased rather to be absent
out of the body and to be at home with the Lord

5:9
Therefore also we are ambitious whether being at home or being away well-pleasing to him to be

5:10
For all of us to be revealed
it behooves before the judgment seat of Christ
that may receive back each the things done in the body according to what he did
whether good or evil

5:11
Knowing therefore the fear of the Lord men we persuade
to God and we have been made manifest
I hope now also in the consciences of you to have been made manifest

5:12
Not again ourselves are we commending to you
but occasion are giving to you of boasting on behalf of us
so that you may have an answer toward those in appearance boasting
and not in the heart

5:13
If for we are beside ourselves it is to God
or if we are sober-minded it is for you

5:14
The for love of Christ compels us
having concluded this that One for all has died
therefore all have died

5:15
And for all He died that those living
no longer to themselves should live but to the One for them having died and having been raised again

5:16
Therefore we from now no one regard according to the flesh
Though even we have regarded according to flesh Christ
yet now no longer we regard Him thus

5:17
Therefore if anyone is in Christ he is a new creation
The old things have passed away
behold has come into being the new

5:18
And all things are of God the One having reconciled us to Himself through Christ
and having given to us the ministry of reconciliation

5:19
how that God was in Christ the world reconciling to Himself
not reckoning to them the trespasses of them
and having put into us the word of reconciliation

5:20
For Christ therefore we are ambassadors as though God is beseeching through us
We implore on behalf of Christ Be reconciled to God

5:21
The One not having known sin
for us sin He made so that we might become the righteousness of God in Him
