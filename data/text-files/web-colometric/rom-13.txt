13:1
Every soul to the authorities being above him let be subject
Not for there is authority if not by God
those however existing by God having been instituted are

13:2
Therefore the one resisting the authority
the of God ordinance has resisted
those now having resisted upon themselves judgment will bring

13:3
The for rulers not are a terror to good works but to evil
Do you desire now not to fear the authority
The good do
and you will have praise from him

13:4
Of God for servant he is to you for good
If however evil you do
be afraid
not for in vain the sword he bears
of God for a servant He is an avenger
for wrath to the one evil doing

13:5
Therefore necessary it is to be subject not only on account of the wrath but also on account of the conscience

13:6
Because of this for also taxes pay you
servants for of God they are
upon this very thing attending continually

13:7
Render to all their dues
to whom the tax the tax to whom the revenue the revenue
to whom the respect the respect to whom the honor the honor

13:8
To no one nothing owe if not one another to love
the one for loving the other the Law has fulfilled

13:9
For Not you shall commit adultery Not you shall murder Not you shall steal Not you shall covet
and if any other commandment
in the word this it is summed up in the saying You shall love the neighbor of you as yourself

13:10
Love to the neighbor evil not does do
the fulfillment therefore of the law love is

13:11
And this knowing the time that it is the hour already for you out of sleep to awaken
now for nearer is of us the salvation
than when first we believed

13:12
The night is nearly over
and the day has drawn near
let us put away therefore the works of darkness
we should put on now the armor of light

13:13
As in daytime properly we should walk
not in reveling and drinking not in sexual immorality and sensuality not in dissension and jealousy

13:14
But put on the Lord Jesus Christ
and of the flesh provision not make for desires
