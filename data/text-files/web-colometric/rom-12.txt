12:1
I exhort therefore you
brothers
through the compassions of God to present the bodies of you a sacrifice living holy well-pleasing to God
which is the reasonable service of you

12:2
And not be conformed to the age this
good and well-pleasing and perfect
but be transformed by the renewing of the mind
for the to prove by you what is will of God

12:3
I say for through the grace having been given to me to everyone being among you not to be high-minded
above what it behooves you to think but to think so as to be sober-minded to each
as God has allotted a measure of faith

12:4
Just as for in one body many members we have
now the members all not the same have function

12:5
so the many one body we are in Christ
and individually one of another members

12:6
We are having however gifts according to the grace having been given to us
different if prophecy according to the proportion of the faith

12:7
or service in the service or teaching in the teaching

12:8
or exhorting in the exhortation giving in generosity leading
with diligence showing mercy with cheerfulness

12:9
Let love be unfeigned
abhorring evil
cleaving to good

12:10
in brotherly love to one another devoted
in honor one another esteeming

12:11
in diligence not lagging
in spirit being fervent
the Lord serving

12:12
in hope rejoicing
in tribulation being patient
in prayer being constant

12:13
to the needs of the saints contributing
hospitality pursuing

12:14
Bless those persecuting Bless
and not curse

12:15
to rejoice with the rejoicing to weep with the weeping

12:16
the same thing toward one another minding
not the things haughty minding
but with the lowly going along Not be
wise in yourselves

12:17
To no one evil for evil repaying
providing right before all men

12:18
if possible of you with all men living at peace

12:19
never yourselves avenging
beloved
instead give place to wrath it has been written for Mine is vengeance I will repay says the Lord

12:20
On the contrary If should hunger the enemy of you
feed him
if he should thirst give drink him
this for doing
coals of fire you will heap upon the head of him

12:21
Not be overcome by evil
but overcome with good evil
