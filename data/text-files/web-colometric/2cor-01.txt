1:1
with the saints all
Paul apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God and Timothy the brother
To the church of God being in Corinth
being in all Achaia

1:2
Grace to you and peace from God the Father of us and the Lord Jesus Christ

1:3
Blessed be the God and Father of the Lord of us Jesus Christ
the Father of compassions and God of all comfort

1:4
the One comforting us in all the tribulation of us
for to be able us to comfort those in every tribulation through the comfort
with which we are comforted ourselves by God

1:5
because as abound the sufferings of the Christ toward us
thus so through Christ abounds also the comfort of us

1:6
If however we are constricted
it is for your comfort and salvation if we are encouraged
it is for your
comfort operating in the endurance of the same sufferings
that also we suffer

1:7
And the hope of us is steadfast for you
knowing that as partners you are of the sufferings
so also of the comfort

1:8
Not for do we want you to be ignorant
brothers
as to the affliction of us having happened in Asia
that against excessively beyond our power we were weighed
so as to despair for us even to live

1:9
But we in ourselves the sentence of death had
in order that not trusting we should be in ourselves
but in God the One raising the dead

1:10
who from such a great a death has delivered us and will deliver us
in whom we have hope
that also still He will deliver us

1:11
joining together also of you for us by supplication
so that by many persons for the toward us grace bestowed through many thanks may be given for us

1:12
For the boasting of us this is the testimony of the conscience of us
that in the holiness and sincerity of God
not in wisdom fleshly but in the grace of God we have conducted ourselves in the world
more abundantly now toward you

1:13
No for other things we write to you other than what you read
or even understand
I hope now that to the end you will understand

1:14
as also you have understood us in part
so that boasting of you we are even as
also you are ours in the day of the Lord of us Jesus

1:15
And with this confidence I was planning previously to you to come
so that a second blessing of grace you might have

1:16
and through you to pass through into Macedonia
and again from Macedonia to come to you
and by you to be set forward to Judea

1:17
Thus therefore purposing not
then lightness did I use
Or what I purpose
according to flesh do I purpose so that there should be with me Yes yes and No no

1:18
Faithful however God is
that the word of us to you not was Yes and No

1:19
The of God for Son Jesus Christ
the One among you by us having been proclaimed
by me and Silvanus and Timothy not was Yes and No
but Yes in Him it has always been

1:20
As many as for promises of God there are in Him is the Yes
Therefore also in Him
the Amen to God is for glory by us

1:21
The One now establishing us with you unto Christ
and having anointed us is God

1:22
the One also having sealed us
and having given the pledge of the Spirit in the hearts of us

1:23
I however as witness God call upon my soul that sparing you
not yet have I come to Corinth

1:24
Not that we lord it over your faith
but fellow workers are of the joy with you
in the for faith you stand firm
