1:1
Paul an apostle of Christ Jesus
According to the command of our God Savior and of Christ Jesus our hope

1:2
To Timothy my true child in the faith;
Grace mercy and peace from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Lord.

1:3
Just as I urged you to remain in Ephesus,
When I was going to Macedonia so that you might warn certain men not to teach other doctrines

1:4
Nor to give heed to myths and genealogies endless,
Which speculations bring rather than stewardship of God which is in

1:5
And the goal of our instruction is love out of a pure heart
And a conscience good and a faith sincere,

1:6
From which some having missed the mark have turned aside to meaningless discourse,

1:7
Desiring to be teachers of the Law not understanding
Neither what they are saying nor that about which they confidently assert.

1:8
We know now that good is the law,
If one it lawfully uses,

1:9
Knowing this that for a righteous one law not is enacted,
For the lawless however and insubordinate for the ungodly and sinful,
For the unholy and profane for murderers of fathers and murderers of mothers for slayers of man,

1:10
For the sexually immoral homosexuals enslavers liars perjurers,
And if anything other in the being sound teaching is opposed to,

1:11
According to the gospel of the glory of the blessed God,
With which have been entrusted I.

1:12
Thankfulness I have for who strengthened me Christ Jesus our Lord that faithful me He esteemed
Having appointed me to service,

1:13
Formerly being a blasphemer and a persecutor and insolent;
But I was shown mercy because being ignorant
I did it in unbelief,

1:14
Surpassingly increased then the grace of our Lord with the faith
And love that are in Christ Jesus.

1:15
Trustworthy is the saying and of full acceptance worthy that Christ Jesus came into the world
Sinners to save;
Of whom the foremost am I,

1:16
But because of this I was shown mercy,
That in me the foremost might display Christ Jesus perfect patience,
As a pattern for those being about to believe on Him to life eternal.

1:17
To now the King of the ages the immortal invisible only God,
Be honor and glory to the ages of the ages Amen.

1:18
This charge I commit to you,
My child Timothy,
According to the going before as to you prophecies,
That you might war by them the good warfare,

1:19
Holding faith and a good conscience,
Which some having cast away
Concerning the faith have caused a shipwreck;

1:20
Among whom are Hymenaeus and Alexander,
Whom I have handed over to Satan,
That they may be disciplined not to blaspheme.
