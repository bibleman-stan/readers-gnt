10:1
For I do not want you
brothers,
to be unaware, brothers, that our
fathers were all under the
cloud and all passed through the sea,

10:2
and all were baptized into Moses in the cloud and in the sea,

10:3
and all ate the same spiritual food

10:4
and all drank the same spiritual drink,
for they were drinking from a spiritual rock that followed them,
and the rock was Christ;

10:5
but God was not pleased with most of them,
for they were laid low in the wilderness.

10:6
Now these things became examples for us, so that we should not be ones who crave evil things,
as they also craved.

10:7
And do not become idolaters,
as some of them were; as it is written:
The people sat down to eat and drink,
and rose up to play.

10:8
And let us not commit sexual immorality,
as some of them committed sexual immorality,
and twenty-three thousand fell in one day.

10:9
And let us not test Christ,
as some of them tested,
and were destroyed by serpents.

10:10
And do not grumble, as some of them grumbled, and were destroyed by the destroyer.

10:11
Now these things happened to them as examples,
and they were written for our instruction,
upon whom the ends of the ages have come.

10:12
Therefore let the one who thinks he stands
take heed lest he fall,

10:13
No temptation has overtaken you except what is common to humanity;
and God is faithful,
who will not allow you to be tempted beyond what you are able,
but with the temptation will also provide the way of escape so that you are able to endure it.

10:14
Therefore,
my beloved,
my beloved, flee from idolatry.

10:15
I speak as to wise people;
judge for yourselves what I say.

10:16
The cup of blessing which we bless, is
it not a sharing in the blood of Christ?
The bread which we break, is it
not a sharing in the body of Christ?

10:17
Because there is one bread,
we who are many are one body,
for we all partake of the one bread.

10:18
Consider Israel according to the flesh:
are not those who eat the sacrifices
sharers of the altar?

10:19
What am I saying
then? That food offered to idols
is anything, or that
an idol is anything?

10:20
No, but that what they sacrifice,
they sacrifice to demons and not to God, and I do not want you to become sharers with demons.

10:21
You are not able to drink the cup of the Lord
and the cup of demons;
you are not able to share in the table of the Lord
and in the table of demons.

10:22
Or shall we provoke the Lord to jealousy?
Are we stronger than he?

10:23
All things are
lawful; but not all things are beneficial.
All things are
lawful; but not all things build up.

10:24
Let no one seek his own good
but the good of the other.

10:25
Eat everything that is sold in the meat market
without raising any question on account of conscience,

10:26
for the earth is the Lord's and its fullness.

10:27
If one of the unbelievers invites you
and you wish to go, eat everything that is set before you
without raising any question on account of conscience;

10:28
but if someone says to you,
This has been offered in sacrifice,
do not eat it, for the sake of the one who informed you
and for conscience --

10:29
conscience, I say, not your own but the other's;
for why is my freedom judged by another's conscience?

10:30
If I partake with thankfulness, why am I denounced for
that over which I give thanks?

10:31
Whether then you eat or drink or whatever you do,
do all things for the glory of God.

10:32
Give no offense to Jews or Greeks or to the church of God,

10:33
just as I also please all in all things,
not seeking my own advantage but that of the many, so that they may be saved.
