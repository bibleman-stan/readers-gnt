10:1
Not I want for you to be ignorant
brothers
that the fathers of us all
under the cloud were
and all through the sea passed

10:2
and all into Moses were baptized in the cloud and in the sea

10:3
and all the same spiritual food ate

10:4
and all the same spiritual drank drink
they were drinking for from the spiritual accompanying them rock
the rock then was Christ

10:5
Nevertheless not with most of them was well pleased God
they were strewn for in the wilderness

10:6
These things now types to us have become for not to be us desirers of evil things
as they also desired

10:7
Neither idolaters are you to be
as some of them as it has been written
Sat down the people to eat and to drink
and rose up to play

10:8
Neither should we commit sexual immorality
as some of them committed sexual immorality
and fell in one day twenty- three thousand

10:9
Neither should we test the Christ
as some of them tested
and by serpents were destroyed

10:10
Neither are you to grumble as some of them grumbled and perished by the Destroyer

10:11
These things now as types happened to them
were written then for admonition of us
to whom the ends of the ages are arrived

10:12
Therefore the one thinking to stand
let him take heed lest he fall

10:13
Temptation you not has seized if not what is common to man
faithful now is God
who not will allow you to be tempted beyond what you are able
but will provide with the temptation also the escape to be able to endure it

10:14
Therefore
beloved of me
flee from idolatry

10:15
As to sensible ones I speak
judge for yourselves what I say

10:16
The cup of blessing that we bless
not a participation is it in the blood of Christ
The bread that we break not
a participation in the body of Christ is it

10:17
Because there is one loaf
one body the many we are
for all of the one loaf we partake

10:18
Consider Israel according to flesh
Not those eating the sacrifices
fellow partakers in the altar are

10:19
What then do I mean
That what is sacrificed to an idol anything is
or that an idol
anything is

10:20
Rather that what sacrifice
to demons and not to God they sacrifice Not I do wish now you fellow partakers with demons to be

10:21
Not you are able the cup of the Lord to drink
and the cup of demons
Not you are able of the table of the Lord
to partake and of the table of demons

10:22
Or do we provoke to jealousy the Lord
Not stronger than He are we

10:23
All things are lawful
but not all things are profitable
All things are lawful
but not all edify

10:24
No one the good of himself let seek
but that of the other

10:25
Everything in the meat market being sold eat
nothing inquiring on account of conscience

10:26
The Lord’s for is the earth and the fullness of it

10:27
If anyone invites you of the unbelieving
and you wish to go everything being set before you eat
nothing inquiring on account of conscience

10:28
If however anyone to you should say
This offered to an idol is
not do eat on account of him having shown it
and conscience

10:29
the conscience now I am saying not your own but that of the other
why for the freedom of me is judged by another’s conscience

10:30
If I with thankfulness partake why am I denounced
for that which I give thanks

10:31
Whether therefore you eat or drink or whatever you do
all things to the glory of God do

10:32
Without offense both to Jews be you and Greeks and to the church of God

10:33
as I also all in all things please
not seeking the of myself profit but that of the many that they may be saved
