3:1
And I
brothers
not was able to speak to you as to spiritual
but as to fleshly as to infants in Christ

3:2
Milk you I gave to drink
not solid food not yet for were you able
In fact not still now are you able

3:3
still for fleshly you are Where
for are among you jealousy and strife not fleshly are you
and according to man are walking

3:4
When for might say one
I indeed am of Paul another
however I of Apollos
not fleshly are you

3:5
Who then is Apollos Who
now is Paul
Servants through whom you believed also to each as the Lord has given

3:6
I planted Apollos watered
but God kept it growing

3:7
So neither the one planting is anything
nor the one watering but only the One giving growth God

3:8
The one planting now and the one watering one are
each now the own reward will receive according to the own labor

3:9
Of God for we are fellow workers
God’s field God’s building you are

3:10
According to the grace of God having been given to me as a wise master builder the foundation I have laid
another however is building upon it Each one
however let take heed how
he builds upon it

3:11
Foundation for another no one is able to lay besides the one being already laid
which is Jesus Christ

3:12
If now anyone builds upon the foundation gold silver stones precious wood hay straw

3:13
of each the work manifest will become
the for day will disclose it
because in fire it is revealed and of each the work what sort it is
the fire itself will prove

3:14
If of anyone the work will remain which he built up
a reward he will receive

3:15
If of anyone the work will be burned up he will suffer loss
he himself however will be saved
so however as through fire

3:16
Not know you that temple of God you yourselves are and the Spirit of God dwells in you

3:17
If anyone the temple of God destroys
will destroy him God
for the temple of God holy is
which are you

3:18
No one himself let deceive
if anyone thinks himself wise to be among you in the age this
foolish let him become so that he may become wise

3:19
The for wisdom of the world this foolishness with God is It has been written
for He is the One catching the wise in the craftiness of them

3:20
And again
The Lord knows the thoughts of the wise
that they are futile

3:21
Therefore no one let boast in men
All things for of you are

3:22
whether things to come all yours
or Paul
or Apollos
or the Cephas
or world
or life
or death
or things present

3:23
you now of Christ
Christ now of God
