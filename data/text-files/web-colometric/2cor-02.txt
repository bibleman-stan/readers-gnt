2:1
I judged but within myself this not again in grief to you to come

2:2
If for I grieve you
again who is gladdening me if not the one being grieved by me

2:3
And I wrote this same thing so that not having come
grief I might have from those of whom it behooves me to rejoice
trusting in all you that my joy of all of you is

2:4
Out of for much affliction and anguish of heart I wrote to you through many tears not
that you might be grieved but the love
that you might know that I have more abundantly toward you

2:5
If however anyone has caused grief
not me has he grieved
but in part
that not I might put it too severely all you

2:6
Sufficient to such a one is the punishment this which is by the majority

2:7
so that on the contrary rather for you to forgive and to comfort him
lest perhaps by more abundant sorrow should be overwhelmed such a one

2:8
Therefore I exhort you to confirm toward him love

2:9
For this indeed also did I write
so that I might know the proof of you whether to everything obedient you are

2:10
To whomever now anything you forgive
I also
and for I to whom I have forgiven if anything
I have forgiven it is for the sake of you in the person of Christ

2:11
so that not we should be outwitted by Satan
not for of his schemes we are ignorant

2:12
Having come now to Troas for the gospel of Christ
also a door to me having been opened in the Lord

2:13
not I had rest in the spirit of me in the not finding my Titus the brother of me
instead having taken leave of them
I went out to Macedonia

2:14
To however God be thanks the One always leading in triumph us in Christ
and the fragrance of the knowledge of him making manifest through us in every place

2:15
For of Christ a sweet perfume we are to God in those being saved
and in those perishing

2:16
to one indeed an odor from death to death
to one now a fragrance from life to life
And for these things who is sufficient

2:17
Not for we are like the many peddling the word of God
but as of sincerity but as of God before God in Christ we speak
