8:1
We make known now to you
brothers
the grace of God having been bestowed
among the churches of Macedonia

8:2
that in much proof of tribulation
the abundance of the joy of them and of deep poverty of them abounded
into the riches the generosity of them

8:3
For according to their ability I testify that and beyond their ability they were willing of themselves

8:4
with much entreaty imploring of us
for the grace and the fellowship of the service among the saints

8:5
And not only as we had hoped
but themselves they gave first to the Lord and then to us by the will of God

8:6
Accordingly exhorted we Titus
that as he had begun so also he might complete with you as well the grace this

8:7
But even as in every way you abound in faith and speech and knowledge and all earnestness
and in the from us to you love
that also in this grace you should abound

8:8
Not as a command do I speak
but through the of others earnestness also the of your love genuineness proving

8:9
You know for the grace of the Lord of us Jesus Christ that for the sake of you He became poor
rich being so that you through that poverty might be enriched

8:10
And a judgment in this matter I give
this indeed for you is profitable
who not only to act
but also to will
have begun from a year ago

8:11
Now then also the doing
complete so that just as there was the readiness of the to will so
also the to complete out of that which you have

8:12
If for the readiness is present
as if he might have acceptable not as not he does have

8:13
Not for that to others there may be ease but for you affliction but of equality

8:14
At the present time your abundance will be for the of those need
so that also their abundance may be for the of you need
so that there may be equality

8:15
As it has been written
He the much not did abound
and he the little not had lack

8:16
Thanks however to God the One putting the same earnestness for you into the heart of Titus

8:17
For our indeed appeal he accepted
very earnest however being of his own accord he has gone out to you

8:18
We have sent now with him the brother
whose praise is in the gospel through all of the churches

8:19
not only now but also having been chosen by the churches
fellow traveler of us with grace this
that is being administered by us toward the himself of the Lord glory and readiness of us

8:20
avoiding this lest anyone us should blame in the abundance this being administered by us

8:21
We are taking great care to do for what is right not only before the Lord but also before men

8:22
We sent with now them the brother of us
whom we have proven in many things often
earnest to be now however much earnest by his confidence more which is in you

8:23
Whether as regards Titus he is partner my and for you a fellow worker
or brothers our they are apostles of the churches the glory of Christ

8:24
Therefore the proof of the love of you and of our boasting about you
to them show in face of the churches
