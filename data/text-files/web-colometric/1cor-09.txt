9:1
Not am I free
Not am I an apostle
Not Jesus the Lord of us have I seen
Not the work of me you are in the Lord

9:2
If to others not I am an apostle
yet at least to you I am
the for seal of my the apostleship you are in the Lord

9:3
My defense to those me examining is this

9:4
No not have we the right to eat and to drink

9:5
No not have we authority a sister a wife to take about
as also the other apostles and the brothers of the Lord and Cephas

9:6
Or only I and Barnabas not have we authority not to work

9:7
Who serves as a soldier at his own expense at any time Who plants a vineyard
and the fruit of it not does eat who shepherds a flock
and from the milk of the flock not does drink

9:8
Not according to man these things do I speak
Or also the law these things not says

9:9
In for the of Moses law it has been written
Not you shall muzzle an ox treading out grain
Not for the oxen is there care with God

9:10
Or because of us entirely is He speaking For sake of us
for it was written because ought in hope the one plowing to plow
and the one threshing in hope to partake

9:11
If we among you spiritual things have sown
is it a great thing if we from you material things will reap

9:12
If others of the over you authority partake
should not more we
But not we did use the right this
Instead all things we bear
so that not any hindrance we should place to the gospel of Christ

9:13
Not know you that those in the temple working the things of the temple eat
those at the altar attending in the altar partake

9:14
So also the Lord has prescribed to those the gospel proclaiming
from the gospel to live

9:15
I however not have used none of these
Neither have I written now these things
that thus it should be with me
it were good for me rather to die
than the boasting of me anyone will make void

9:16
If for I preach the gospel
nothing there is to me boasting necessity
for me is laid upon Woe
however to me be
if not I should preach the gospel

9:17
If for willingly this I do
a reward I have
if however unwillingly
a stewardship I am entrusted with

9:18
What then of me is the reward
That in preaching the gospel free of charge I should offer the gospel
so as not to use up the right of me in the gospel

9:19
Free for being from all to all myself I became servant
so that the more I might gain

9:20
And I became to the Jews like a Jew
so that Jews I might win
To those under the Law as under the law not being myself under the law so that those under the law I might win

9:21
To those outside the Law like outside the Law not being outside the law of God but under the law to Christ
so that I might win those outside the Law

9:22
I became to the weak weak
that the weak I might win
These things to all I have become all
so that by all means some I might save

9:23
All things now I do on account of the gospel
that a fellow partaker with it I might become

9:24
Not know you that those in a race course running
all indeed run
one however receives the prize
Thus run that you might obtain it

9:25
Everyone now striving in all things controls himself they
indeed then that a perishable crown they might receive
we however an imperishable

9:26
I therefore thus run as not uncertainly so I fight as not the air beating

9:27
But I batter of me the body and bring it into servitude
not hardly to others having preached myself disqualified I might be
