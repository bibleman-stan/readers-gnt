4:1
I say now for as long as time the heir a child is,
Not he differs from a slave
Though owner of everything being,

4:2
Instead under guardians he is and trustees,
Until the time appointed by his father.

4:3
So also we when we were children,
Under the basic principles of the world were held in bondage;

4:4
But when had come the fullness of the time,
Sent forth God the Son of him having been born of a woman,
Having been born under the Law,

4:5
That those under the Law He might redeem,
So that the divine adoption as sons we might receive.

4:6
Because now you are sons,
Sent forth God the Spirit of his Son into our hearts,
Crying out;
Abba Father.

4:7
So no longer you are a slave but a son;
If now a son,
Also an heir through God.

4:8
But at that time indeed not knowing God
You were enslaved to those by nature not being gods;

4:9
Now however having known God,
Rather however having been known by God how do you turn again to the weak and destitute principles,
To which again anew enslaved you desire?

4:10
Days you observe and months and seasons and years.

4:11
I fear for you lest perhaps in vain I have toiled for you.

4:12
Become as I am because I also have become as you,
Brothers,
I implore you.
In nothing me have You wronged;

4:13
You know now that in weakness of the flesh I proclaimed the gospel to you at the first,

4:14
And the test of you in my flesh not you did despise me nor reject me with contempt,
But as an angel of God you received me even as Christ Jesus.

4:15
What then the blessedness of you?
I bear witness for to you that if possible the eyes of you having gouged out you would have given them to me.

4:16
So enemy of you have I become speaking truth to you?

4:17
They are zealous after you not rightly,
But to exclude you from us They desire so that them you might be zealous after.

4:18
Good it is now to be zealous in a right thing at all times
And not only in being present my with you,

4:19
My Children,
Of whom again I travail until
That shall have been formed Christ in you;

4:20
I was wishing indeed to be present with you presently and to change my tone,
Because I am perplexed as to you.

4:21
Tell me,
Those under the Law wishing to be,
The Law not you do listen to?

4:22
It has been written for that Abraham two sons had one
Of the slave woman and one of the free;

4:23
But the one indeed of the slave woman according to flesh has been born,
The one but of the free through promise.

4:24
Which things are allegorized;
These for are two covenants,
One indeed from Mount Sinai unto slavery begetting,
Which is Hagar,

4:25
And Hagar Sinai Mount is in Arabia,
She corresponds now to the present Jerusalem she is in slavery
For with the children of her;

4:26
But the above Jerusalem free is,
Who is our mother;

4:27
It has been written for Rejoice,
O barren woman the one not bearing,
Break forth and call aloud,
The one not travailing;
Because many are the children of the desolate woman more than of her having the husband.

4:28
You now,
Brothers,
Like Isaac of promise children are;

4:29
But just as at that time the one according to flesh having been born was persecuting the one born according to Spirit so also it is now.

4:30
But what says the Scripture Cast out the slave woman and the son of her,
No for not will inherit the son of the slave woman
Along with the son of the free.

4:31
So then,
Brothers,
Not we are of the slave woman children but of the free.
