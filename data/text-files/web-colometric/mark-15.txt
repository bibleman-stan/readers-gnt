15:1
And early in the morning a counsel held
the chief priests with the elders and scribes and all the Council
having bound Jesus they led Him away
and delivered Him to Pilate

15:2
And questioned Him Pilate
You are the King of the Jews
And answering to him He says You have said

15:3
And were accusing Him the chief priests of many things

15:4
And Pilate again began to question Him saying
Not answer You nothing See how many things You they testify against

15:5
But Jesus no longer nothing answered
so as to amaze Pilate

15:6
At then the feast he used to release to them one prisoner whom they requested

15:7
There was then the one called Barabbas
with the rebels having been bound
who in the insurrection murder had committed

15:8
And having cried out the crowd began to beg him to do as usually he did for them

15:9
But Pilate answered them saying
Wish you that I should release to you the King of the Jews

15:10
He was aware for that because of envy had delivered up Him the chief priests

15:11
But the chief priests stirred up the crowd
so that instead Barabbas he might release to them

15:12
And Pilate again answering was saying to them
What then want I should do
to him you call the King of the Jews

15:13
And again they cried out
Crucify Him

15:14
And Pilate was saying to them
What indeed did He commit evil
But much more they shouted
Crucify Him

15:15
And Pilate desiring to the crowd that which was satisfactory to do
released to them Barabbas
and he delivered Jesus having flogged him that He might be crucified

15:16
And the soldiers led away him into the palace
that is the Praetorium
and they call together all the cohort

15:17
And they put on Him purple
and placed on Him
having twisted together of thorns a crown

15:18
and they began to salute Him
Hail King of the Jews

15:19
And they kept striking His head with a reed
and spitting on Him
and bending the knees
they were kneeling down to him

15:20
And when they had mocked Him
they took off him the purple
and put on Him the garments his own
And they are leading out him that they might crucify Him

15:21
And they compel passing by one Simon of Cyrene coming from the country
the father of Alexander and Rufus
that he might carry the cross of him

15:22
And they bring Him to Golgotha a place
which is translated of a Skull Place

15:23
And they were offering Him having been mixed with myrrh wine
He however not did take it

15:24
And having crucified Him
also they divided the garments of him
casting lots for them who what should take

15:25
It was then the hour third
and they crucified Him

15:26
And there was the inscription of the accusation against him having been written The King of the Jews

15:27
And with him they crucify two robbers
one at the right hand and one at the left of him

15:29
And those passing by were railing at Him
shaking the heads of them
and saying
Aha The One destroying the temple
and building it in three days

15:30
save Yourself having descended from the cross

15:31
Likewise also the chief priests mocking among one another with the scribes were saying
Others He saved
Himself not He is able to save

15:32
The Christ the King of Israel let Him descend now from the cross that we might see
and believe
And those being crucified with him were upbraiding Him

15:33
And having arrived the hour sixth
darkness came over all the land until the hour ninth

15:34
And at the ninth hour cried out Jesus in a voice loud Eloi Eloi lema sabachthani
Which is translated God of me God of me
to why have You forsaken Me

15:35
And some of those standing by having heard were saying
Behold Elijah He calls

15:36
Having run then one and having filled a sponge with vinegar
having put it on a reed gave to drink him saying
Let be let us see if comes Elijah to take down him

15:37
But Jesus having uttered a cry loud breathed His last

15:38
And the veil of the temple was torn into two from top to bottom

15:39
Having seen then the centurion
standing from opposite of him that thus He breathed His last
he said
Truly this man the Son of God was

15:40
There were then also women
from afar off looking on
among whom also Mary Magdalene
and Mary the of James the least and of Joseph mother and Salome

15:41
who when He was in Galilee
had been following Him
and had been ministering to him and other many those having come up with him to Jerusalem

15:42
And already evening having arrived since it was the Preparation
that is the day before Sabbath

15:43
having come Joseph from Arimathea a prominent Council member
who also himself was waiting for the kingdom of God
having boldness went in to Pilate
and asked for the body of Jesus

15:44
And Pilate wondered if already He were dead
And having called to him the centurion
he questioned him whether already He had died

15:45
And having known it from the centurion He granted the body to Joseph

15:46
And having bought a linen cloth
having taken down him
he wrapped Him in the linen cloth
and laid Him in tomb
which was cut out of a rock
And he rolled a stone to the door of the tomb

15:47
And Mary Magdalene and Mary the mother of Joseph were watching where He was laid
