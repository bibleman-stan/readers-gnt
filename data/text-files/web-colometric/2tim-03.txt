3:1
But this realize that in the last days will be present times difficult;

3:2
Will be for men lovers of self lovers of money boastful,
Proud abusive to parents disobedient ungrateful unholy,

3:3
Unloving implacable slanderous without self-control savage without love of good,

3:4
Treacherous reckless puffed up lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God,

3:5
Having a form of godliness
But the power of it denying;
And these turn away from.

3:6
Out of this sort for are those entering into households
And taking captive weak women burdened with sins,
Being led away by passions various,

3:7
Always learning and never to a knowledge of the truth to come being able.

3:8
By which way now Jannes and Jambres opposed Moses,
So also these oppose the truth,
Men being depraved in mind disqualified regarding the faith.

3:9
But not they will advance further much,
For the folly of them plain will be to all as also that of those two became.

3:10
But you have closely followed my teaching conduct purpose,
Faith patience love endurance,

3:11
Persecutions sufferings,
Such as to me happened in Antioch in Iconium in Lystra,
What manner of persecutions I endured;
And yet out of all me delivered the Lord.

3:12
Also all now desiring to live piously in Christ Jesus will be persecuted;

3:13
But evil men and impostors will advance to worse,
Deceiving and being deceived.

3:14
But you abide in the things you have learned and have been assured of,
Having known from whom you learned them,

3:15
And that from childhood sacred writings you have known being able you to make wise unto salvation
Through faith in Christ Jesus;

3:16
Every Scripture is God-breathed and profitable for instruction,
For conviction for correction and for training in righteousness,

3:17
So that complete may be the of God man,
Toward every work good having been fully equipped.
