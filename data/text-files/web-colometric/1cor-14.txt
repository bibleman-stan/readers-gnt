14:1
Earnestly pursue love
earnestly desire now spiritual gifts
especially now that you might prophesy

14:2
The one for speaking in a tongue not to men speaks
but to God no one for hears
in the Spirit however he utters mysteries

14:3
The one however prophesying to men speaks for edification and encouragement and consolation

14:4
The one speaking in a tongue himself edifies
the one however prophesying the church edifies

14:5
I desire now all you to speak in tongues
rather however that you should prophesy greater is
now the one prophesying than the one speaking in tongues except if not he should interpret
so that the church edification might receive

14:6
Now however
brothers
if I should come to you
in tongues speaking what you will I profit
if not to you I should speak
either in revelation
or in knowledge
or in prophecy
or of teaching

14:7
Even lifeless things a sound giving
whether flute or harp
if distinction to the sounds not they give
how will it be known what is being piped
or being harped

14:8
Also for if indistinct sound the trumpet gives
who will prepare himself for battle

14:9
So also you with the tongue
if not intelligible speech you give
how will it be known what is being spoken
You will be for into the air speaking

14:10
So many if it may be
kinds of languages there are in the world
and none without meaning

14:11
If therefore not I know the power of the sound I will be to the one speaking
a foreigner and the one speaking to me a foreigner

14:12
So also you
since zealous you are of spiritual gifts
for the edification of the church search that you should cause it to abound

14:13
Therefore the one speaking in a tongue let him pray
that he might interpret

14:14
If for I pray in a tongue
the spirit of me prays
but the mind of me unfruitful is

14:15
What then is it
I will pray with the spirit I will pray
however also with the mind
I will sing praise with the spirit
I will sing praise however also with the mind

14:16
Otherwise if you bless spirit
the one filling the place of the uninstructed
how will he say the Amen at your thanksgiving
since what you say not he knows

14:17
You truly for well are giving thanks
but the other not is edified

14:18
I thank God than all of you more in tongues speaking

14:19
but in the church I desire five words with the mind of me to speak
that also others I might instruct
rather than ten thousand words in a tongue

14:20
Brothers
not children be in the minds
Yet in the evil be little children
in the however thinking full grown be

14:21
In the law it has been written By other tongues
and by lips other I will speak to the people this
and not even thus will they hear Me says the Lord

14:22
So then tongues for a sign are not to those believing
but to the unbelieving but prophecy is not to the unbelieving but to the believing

14:23
If therefore gathers the church whole together the place
and all should speak in tongues
come in however uninstructed ones or unbelievers not will they say that you are mad

14:24
If however all prophesy
should come in then some unbeliever or uninstructed he is convicted by all he is examined by all

14:25
the secrets of the heart of him manifest become
And thus having fallen upon his face he will worship God
declaring that certainly God among you is

14:26
What then is it
brothers
When you may come together each a psalm has
a teaching has a revelation has a tongue has an interpretation has
All things for edification let be done

14:27
If with a tongue anyone speaks
let it be by two or the most three and in turn and one let interpret

14:28
If however not there is an interpreter
let him be silent in the church
to himself then let him speak and to God

14:29
Prophets then two or three let speak
and the others let discern

14:30
If however to another a revelation should be made sitting by
the first let be silent

14:31
You are able for one by one all to prophesy so that all may learn
and all may be exhorted

14:32
And spirits of prophets to prophets are subject

14:33
Not for He is of disorder the God
but of peace As in all the churches of the saints

14:34
the women in the churches let them be silent
Not for it is allowed to them to speak
but to be in submission
as also the law says

14:35
If however anything to learn
they desire at home the own husbands
let them ask shameful for it is for a woman to speak in the church

14:36
Or from you the word of God has gone out
Or to you only has it come

14:37
If anyone considers a prophet himself to be or spiritual
let him recognize the things I write to you that of the Lord are

14:38
If however anyone is ignorant let him be ignored

14:39
So
brothers of me
earnestly desire to prophesy
and to speak
not do forbid in tongues

14:40
All things however properly and with order let be done
