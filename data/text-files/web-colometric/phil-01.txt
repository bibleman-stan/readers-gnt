1:1
Paul and Timothy servants of Christ Jesus
To all the saints in Christ Jesus being in Philippi
With the overseers and deacons;

1:2
Grace to you and peace from our God Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

1:3
I my thank God upon every remembrance of you

1:4
Always in every my supplication for all of you with joy the supplication making,

1:5
For the partnership of you in the gospel from the first day until now,

1:6
Being persuaded of the very thing this
That who begun in you a work good
Will complete it until the day of Christ Jesus;

1:7
Accordingly it is right for me
This to feel about all of you,
Since have I in the heart you in my both chains
And in the defense and confirmation of the gospel
Fellow partakers with me of grace all you are;

1:8
Witness my for God is how I long after all you in the affection of Christ Jesus.

1:9
And this I pray that the love of you yet more and more may abound
In knowledge and all discernment,

1:10
For to approve you the things being excellent,
So that you may be pure and blameless unto the day of Christ,

1:11
Being filled with the fruit of righteousness that is through Jesus Christ
To the glory and praise of God.

1:12
To know now you I want,
Brothers,
That the things concerning me really
To the advancement of the gospel have turned out,

1:13
So as my for chains clearly known in Christ to have become
In all the palace guard and to the rest all,

1:14
And most of the brothers
In the Lord trusting my by chains more abundantly to dare fearlessly the word to speak.

1:15
Some indeed even from envy and strife,
Some however also from goodwill
Christ are proclaiming;

1:16
The ones indeed out of love knowing that for defense of the gospel I am appointed,

1:17
The ones however out of selfish ambition Christ are proclaiming,
Not purely supposing tribulation to add my to chains.

1:18
What then Only that in every way whether in pretext or in truth Christ is proclaimed,
And in this I rejoice;
Yes and I will rejoice,

1:19
I know for that this for me will turn out to deliverance your through prayer
And the provision of the Spirit of Jesus Christ,

1:20
According to the earnest expectation and my hope that in nothing I will be ashamed,
Whether by life or by death.
[...]
,

1:21
To me for to live
Is Christ and to die is gain.

1:22
If I am however to live in flesh,
This for me is the fruit
And what shall I choose Not I know;

1:23
I am pressed however between the two,
The desire having for myself to depart
And with Christ to be,
Very much for more better,

1:24
But to remain in the flesh
Is more necessary for the sake of you.

1:25
And this having been persuaded of
I know that I will remain and will continue with all of you
Your for progress and joy of the faith,

1:26
So that the boasting of you may abound to Christ Jesus in me
My through coming again to you.

1:27
Only worthily of the gospel of Christ conduct yourselves so that whether having come and having seen you
Or being absent I might hear the things concerning you that you are standing firm in one spirit,
With one mind striving together for the faith of the gospel,

1:28
And not being frightened in nothing
By those opposing you which is to them a demonstration of destruction,
To you however of salvation and this from God,

1:29
For to you it has been granted concerning Christ not only in Him to believe
But also concerning Him to suffer,

1:30
The same conflict having
Such as you saw in me
And now hear of in me.
