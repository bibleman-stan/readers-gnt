8:1
Concerning now the things sacrificed to idols we know indeed all knowledge we have
Knowledge puffs up
but love builds up

8:2
If anyone thinks to have known anything
not yet does he know as it is necessary to know

8:3
If however anyone loves God
he is known by Him

8:4
Concerning the eating therefore of the things sacrificed to idols we know that nothing an idol is in the world
and that there is no God if not one

8:5
Even truly if indeed there are
those called gods whether in heaven or on earth as there are gods many
and lords many

8:6
yet to us there is one God the Father
of whom are the things all and we for Him and one Lord Jesus Christ
by whom are the things all and we through Him

8:7
But not in all is this knowledge
Some now by habit until now of the idol as of a thing sacrificed to an idol eat
and the conscience of them weak being it is defiled

8:8
Food however us not will commend to God
neither for if we should eat have we an advantage
nor if not we should eat do we come short

8:9
Be careful however lest somehow the right of you this an occasion of stumbling becomes to those being weak

8:10
If for anyone sees you having knowledge
in an idol’s temple eating not the conscience of him
weak being will be built up so as the things sacrificed to idols to eat

8:11
Is destroyed for the one being weak
through your knowledge the brother
for whom Christ died

8:12
Thus then sinning against the brothers
and wounding their conscience
being weak against Christ you sin

8:13
Therefore if food snares the brother of me
never not shall I eat meat to the age
so that not the brother of me I might snare
