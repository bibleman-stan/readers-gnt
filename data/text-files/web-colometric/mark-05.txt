5:1
And they came to the other side of the sea to the region of the Gerasenes

5:2
And having gone forth of him out of the boat
immediately met Him out of the tombs a man with a unclean spirit

5:3
who the dwelling had in the tombs
And not even with chains no longer anyone was able him to bind

5:4
because that he often with shackles and chains had been bound
and had been torn in two by him the chains
and the shackles had been shattered
and no one was able him to subdue

5:5
And constantly all night and day in the tombs and in the mountains he was crying out
and cutting himself with stones

5:6
And having seen Jesus from afar he ran
and fell on his knees before him

5:7
And having cried in a voice loud he said
What to me and to You Jesus Son of God the Most High
I adjure You by God not me torment

5:8
He was saying for to him
You come forth the unclean spirit out of the man

5:9
And He was asking him
What is the name of you
And saying he answered Legion
is name to me because many we are

5:10
And he begged Him numerous times
that not them He would send out of the country

5:11
There was now there near the mountain a herd of pigs great feeding

5:12
And they begged Him saying
Send us into the pigs
so that into them we may enter

5:13
And He allowed them
And having gone out the unclean spirits entered into the pigs
and rushed the herd down the steep bank into the sea about two thousand
and they were drowned in the sea

5:14
And those feeding them fled
and proclaimed it to the city and to the country
And they went out to see
what it is that has been done

5:15
And they come to Jesus
and see the man possessed by demons
sitting clothed and sound minded
the one having had the legion
and they were afraid

5:16
And related to them those having seen it how it happened to the one being possessed by demons
and concerning the pigs

5:17
And they began to implore Him to depart from the region of them

5:18
And having entered He into the boat
was begging Him the one having been possessed by demons
that with him he might be

5:19
And not He did permit him
but He says to him
Go to the home of you to your own
and report to them
how much the Lord for you did
and had mercy on you

5:20
And he departed and began to proclaim in the Decapolis
how much had done for him Jesus
and all were marveling

5:21
And having passed over Jesus in the boat again to the other side
was gathered a crowd great to him
and He was beside the sea

5:22
And comes one of the synagogue rulers
named Jairus
and having seen Him
falls at the feet of him

5:23
and he begs Him much
saying The little daughter of me at the end is holding that having come
You would lay the hands on her so that she might be cured and she shall live

5:24
And He departed with him
And was following Him a crowd great
and pressing in on Him

5:25
And a woman being with a flux of blood twelve years

5:26
and much having suffered under many physicians
and having spent the of her all
and in no way having benefited
but rather to the worse having come

5:27
having heard concerning Jesus
having come up in the crowd behind
she touched the clothing of him

5:28
She was saying for If I shall touch even the garments of him I will be healed

5:29
And immediately was dried up the flow of the blood of her
and she knew in the body that she was healed from the affliction

5:30
And immediately Jesus having known in Himself
the out of him power having gone forth
having turned in the crowd said
Who of me touched the garments

5:31
And were saying to him the disciples of him
You see the crowd pressing in on You
and say You Who Me touched

5:32
And He was looking around to see the one this having done

5:33
And the woman having been frightened and trembling knowing
what had been done to her came
and fell down before him
and told Him all the truth

5:34
And He said to her
Daughter the faith of you has healed you
go in peace
and be sound from the affliction of you

5:35
While yet He is speaking they come from the ruler of synagogue’s house
saying The daughter of You is dead
why still trouble you the Teacher

5:36
And Jesus having heard
the word spoken
says to the ruler of the synagogue
Not fear only believe

5:37
And not He allowed no one with him to follow
if not Peter and James and John the brother of James

5:38
And they come to the house of the ruler of the synagogue
and He beholds a commotion and weeping and wailing much

5:39
And having entered He says to them
Why make you commotion and weep
The child not is dead but sleeps

5:40
And they were laughing at Him
He then having put out all
takes with him the father of the child
and the mother and those with him
and enters in
where was the child

5:41
And having taken the hand of the child
He says to her
Talitha koum
Which is translated
Little girl to you I say arise

5:42
And immediately arose the girl
and began walking
for she was of years twelve
And they were overcome immediately with amazement great

5:43
And He instructed them strictly that no one should know this
and He commanded to be given to her to eat
