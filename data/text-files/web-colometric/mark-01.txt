1:1
The beginning of the gospel of Jesus Christ

1:2
As it has been written in Isaiah the prophet
Behold I send the messenger of me before face of You
who will prepare the way of You

1:3
The voice of one crying in the wilderness
Prepare the way of the Lord
straight make the paths of him

1:4
Came John baptizing in the wilderness
proclaiming a baptism of repentance for forgiveness of sins

1:5
And were going out to him all the of Judea region and of Jerusalem all
and were being baptized by him in the Jordan river
confessing the sins of them

1:6
And was John
clothed in hair of a camel
and a belt of leather around the waist of him
and he is eating locusts and wild honey

1:7
And he was preaching saying He comes who is mightier than I after me
of whom not I am sufficient having stooped down to untie the strap of the sandals of him

1:8
I baptized you with water
He however will baptize you with the Holy Spirit

1:9
And it came to pass in those days
that came Jesus from Nazareth of Galilee
and was baptized in the Jordan by John

1:10
And immediately going up from the water he saw
tearing open the heavens
and the Spirit as a dove descending upon him

1:11
And a voice came out of the heavens
You are the Son of me the beloved
in You I am well pleased

1:12
And immediately the Spirit Him drives out into the wilderness

1:13
And He was in the wilderness forty days
being tempted by Satan And He was with the wild animals
and the angels were ministering to him

1:14
And after the delivering up of John
came Jesus into Galilee
proclaiming the gospel of God

1:15
and saying Has been fulfilled the time and has drawn near the kingdom of God
repent
and believe in the gospel

1:16
And passing by the Sea of Galilee He saw Simon and Andrew the brother of Simon
casting a net into the sea
for they were fishermen

1:17
And said to them Jesus
Come after Me and I will make you to become fishers of men

1:18
And immediately having left the nets
they followed Him

1:19
And having gone on a little He saw James the son of Zebedee and John the brother of him
and they were in the boat adjusting the nets

1:20
And immediately He called them
and having left the father of them Zebedee in the boat with the hired servants
they went away after him

1:21
And they go into Capernaum
and immediately on the Sabbaths He was teaching
into the synagogue

1:22
And they were astonished at the teaching of him
for He was teaching them as having authority
and not as the scribes

1:23
And immediately there was in the synagogue of them a man with a unclean spirit and he cried out

1:24
saying
What to us and to You Jesus of Nazareth
Did You come to destroy us
I know You who are the Holy One of God

1:25
And rebuked him Jesus saying
Be silent
and come forth out of him

1:26
And having thrown into convulsions him
the unclean spirit and having cried in a voice loud it came forth out of him

1:27
And were astonished all
so as to question among themselves saying
What is this
teaching new
With authority even the unclean spirits He commands
and they obey Him

1:28
And went out the news of him immediately everywhere into all the surrounding region of Galilee

1:29
And immediately out of the synagogue having gone forth they came into the house of Simon and Andrew
with James and John

1:30
and the mother-in-law of Simon was laying sick fevering And immediately they speak to him about her

1:31
And having come to her He raised up her
having taken hold of the hand
And left her the fever and she began to minister to them

1:32
Evening now having come when set the sun
they began bringing to him all those sick being
and those being possessed by demons

1:33
And was all the city gathered together at the door

1:34
And He healed many sick being of various diseases
and demons many He cast out
And not He would allow to speak the demons
because they knew Him

1:35
And very early in night still much having risen up He went out
and departed into solitary a place
and there He was praying

1:36
And went after him Simon and those with him

1:37
and having found Him
also they say to him Everyone seeks You

1:38
And He says to them
Let us go another way into the neighboring
towns so that also there I might preach
for this therefore have I come forth

1:39
And He was going preaching in the synagogues of them in all Galilee
and demons casting out

1:40
And comes to him a leper imploring Him and kneeling down to him
saying to him If You are willing
You are able me to cleanse

1:41
And having been angry having stretched out the hand of him He touched him
and says to him
I am willing be you cleansed

1:42
And immediately departed from him the leprosy
and he was cleansed

1:43
And having sternly warned him
immediately He sent away him

1:44
And He says to him
See that nothing to none you speak
But go
yourself show to the priest
and offer for the cleansing of you
what commanded Moses
for a testimony to them

1:45
And having gone out he began to proclaim much and to spread abroad the matter
so that no longer He was able openly into the city to enter
but out in solitary places He was
and they were coming to him from every quarter
