1:1
Paul an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God and Timothy the brother

1:2
To the in Colossae saints and faithful brothers in Christ;
Grace to you and peace from our God Father.

1:3
We give thanks to God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ continually
For you praying,

1:4
Having heard of the faith of you in Christ Jesus and the love
That you have toward all the saints

1:5
Because of the hope being laid up for you in the heavens,
Which you heard of before in the word of truth the gospel

1:6
The one being present unto you,
Just as also in all the world
It is bearing fruit and increasing just as also among you
From the day you heard
And knew the grace of God in truth;

1:7
Just as you learned from Epaphras the beloved fellow bond-servant our,
Who is faithful on behalf our a servant of Christ,

1:8
The one also having made known to us your love in the Spirit.

1:9
Because of this also we
From the day we heard,
In all wisdom and understanding spiritual,
[...]
And his

1:10
To walk worthily of the Lord in all pleasing
In every work good bringing forth fruit
And growing in the knowledge of God,

1:11
With all power being strengthened according to the might glorious his
Unto all endurance and patience with joy,

1:12
Giving thanks to the Father
Who qualified you for the share of the inheritance of the saints in the light,

1:13
Who has delivered us from the dominion of darkness
And transferred us into the kingdom of the Son beloved his,

1:14
In whom we have redemption the forgiveness of sins;

1:15
He is the image of God invisible the firstborn over all creation,

1:16
Because in Him were created all things in the heavens and upon the earth,
The visible and the invisible,
Whether thrones
Or dominions
Or rulers
Or authorities;
All things through Him and unto Him have been created;

1:17
And He is before all things
And all things in Him hold together,

1:18
And He is the head of the body the church;
Who is beginning firstborn out from the dead,
So that might be in all things He holding preeminence,

1:19
Because in Him was pleased all the fullness to dwell

1:20
And by Him to reconcile all things to Himself,
Having made peace by the blood of the cross his through Him
Whether the things on the earth or the things in the heavens;

1:21
And you once being alienated
And hostile in mind in the deeds

1:22
Now however you are reconciled in the body of the flesh his through
To present you holy and unblemished and blameless before Him,

1:23
If indeed you continue in the faith
Established and firm
And not being moved away from the hope of the gospel that you have heard,
Having been proclaimed in all creation under heaven,
Of which have become I Paul a minister.

1:24
Now I rejoice in the sufferings for you,
And I am filling up that which is lacking of the tribulations of Christ in my flesh
For the body his,
Which is the church,

1:25
Of which became I a minister according to the administration of God having been given me toward you
To complete the word of God,

1:26
The mystery having been hidden from the ages and from the
Now however having been manifested to the saints his,

1:27
To whom has willed God to make known what is the riches of the glory of the mystery this among the Gentiles which is
Christ in you the hope of glory;

1:28
Whom we preach admonishing every man
And teaching every man in all wisdom,
So that we may present every man perfect in Christ;

1:29
Unto this also I toil striving according to the energy his
Working in me in power.
