1:1
The elder To Gaius the beloved
whom I love in truth

1:2
Beloved
concerning all things I pray you to prosper and to be in good health
just as prospers your soul

1:3
I rejoiced for exceedingly coming of the brothers and bearing witness of your truth
just as you in truth are walking

1:4
Greater than these things not I have joy
that I should hear of my children in the truth walking

1:5
Beloved
faithfully you are doing whatever if you might have done toward the brothers
and they are strangers

1:6
who testified of your love before the church
whom well you will do having set forward worthily of God

1:7
On behalf for of the name they went forth
nothing accepting from the Gentiles

1:8
We therefore ought to receive such men
so that fellow workers we may be in the truth

1:9
I have written something to the church
but the one loving to be first among them Diotrephes not welcomes us

1:10
Because of this if I might come
I will bring to remembrance of him the works which he is doing
with words evil prating against us
and not being satisfied with these
neither himself he receives the brothers
and those purposing he forbids
and from the church he casts them out

1:11
Beloved
not do imitate what is evil but what is good
The one doing good of God is
the one doing evil not has seen God

1:12
To Demetrius witness has been given by all and by itself the truth
and we also bear witness
and you know that the testimony of us true is

1:13
Many things I had to write to you
but not I desire with ink and pen to you to write

1:14
I hope however soon you to see
and mouth to mouth we will speak

1:15
Peace to you
Greet you the friends
Greet the friends by name
