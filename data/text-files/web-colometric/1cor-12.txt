12:1
Concerning now spiritual gifts
brothers
not I do want you to be ignorant

12:2
You know that when pagans you were
to idols mute even as you were led being carried away

12:3
Therefore I make known to you that no one in the Spirit of God speaking says
Accursed is Jesus
and no one is able to say
Lord is Jesus if not in the Holy Spirit

12:4
Varieties now of gifts there are
but the same Spirit

12:5
and varieties of services there are
but the same Lord

12:6
and varieties of workings there are
the but same God is working the all things in everyone

12:7
To each now is given the manifestation of the Spirit for the common profiting

12:8
To one truly for through the Spirit is given a word of wisdom
to another now a word of knowledge according to the same Spirit

12:9
and to a different one faith by the same Spirit to another gifts of healing in that one Spirit

12:10
to another working of miracles
to another prophecy to another distinguishing of spirits and to a different one various kinds of tongues
to another interpretation of tongues

12:11
All now these things works the one and the same Spirit apportioning individually to each as He wills

12:12
Just as for the body one is
and members many has
all now the members of the body many being one are body
so also is Christ

12:13
Also for in one Spirit we all into one body were baptized
whether Jews
or Greeks
whether slaves
or free and all one Spirit we were made to drink

12:14
Also for the body not is one member but many

12:15
If should say the foot
Because not I am a hand
not I am of the body
not on account of this not is it of the body

12:16
And if should say the ear
Because not I am an eye
not I am of the body
not on account of this not is it of the body

12:17
If all the body were an eye
where would be the hearing
If all were hearing where
would be the sense of smell

12:18
now however God has arranged the members one each of them in the body as He desired

12:19
If now were all one member
where would be the body

12:20
Now however many even are the members
one however body

12:21
Not is able now the eye to say to the hand
Need of you not I have
Or again the head to the feet
Need of you not I have

12:22
But much rather those seeming members of the body weaker to be indispensable are

12:23
and those which we think less honorable to be of the body
these honor more abundant we bestow
and the unpresentable parts of us decorum more abundant have

12:24
and the presentable parts of us no need have
But God has composed the body
to the parts lacking more abundant having given honor

12:25
that not there should be division in the body
but the same for one another should have concern the members

12:26
And if suffers one member
suffer with it all the members
if is honored member
rejoice with it all the members

12:27
You now are the body of Christ and members in particular

12:28
And some indeed has appointed God in the church first apostles
secondly prophets third teachers
then miracles then gifts
of healing helping administrating various kinds of tongues

12:29
Not are all apostles
Not all prophets
Not all teachers
Not have all miracles

12:30
Not all gifts have of healings
Not all in tongues do speak
Not all do interpret

12:31
Be desirous of however the gifts greater
And now according to a more surpassing way to you I show
