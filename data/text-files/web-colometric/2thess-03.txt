3:1
Finally pray,
Brothers,
For us that the word of the Lord may spread quickly and may be glorified
Just as also with you,

3:2
And that we may be delivered from perverse and evil men,
Not for all are of the faith.

3:3
But faithful is the Lord,
Who will strengthen you
And will keep you from evil.

3:4
We are persuaded now in the Lord as to you
That the things that we command both you are doing and you will do.

3:5
And the Lord may direct your hearts into the love of God
And into the steadfastness of Christ.

3:6
We command now you,
Brothers,
In the name of our Lord Jesus Christ are to withdraw you from every brother idly walking
And not according to the tradition
That received from us.

3:7
For yourselves you know how it behooves you to imitate us,
Because not we were idle among you

3:8
Nor without payment bread did we eat from anyone,
But in labor and hardship
Night and day working in order not to be burdensome to any of you;

3:9
Not that not we have the right,
But that ourselves as an example we may offer to you for you to imitate us.

3:10
For even when we were with you,
This we were commanding you that if anyone not is willing to work neither let him eat.

3:11
We hear for some are walking among you idly not at all working
But being busybodies;

3:12
To now such we warn and exhort by our Lord Jesus Christ,
So that with quietness working their own bread they may eat.

3:13
You now,
Brothers,
Not shall grow weary in well-doing.

3:14
If then anyone not obey this instruction our through the letter of this man take note,
Not to mix with him so that he may be ashamed;

3:15
And yet not as an enemy esteem him,
But admonish him as a brother.

3:16
Himself now the Lord of peace may give you peace through all in every way The Lord be
With all of you.

3:17
The greeting in my own hand Paul,
Which is my sign in every letter In this manner I write.

3:18
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with all of you.
