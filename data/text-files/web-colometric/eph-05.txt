5:1
Be therefore imitators of God as children beloved,

5:2
And walk in love just as also Christ loved us
And gave up himself for us as an offering and a sacrifice to God into an aroma of a sweet smell.

5:3
Sexual immorality however and impurity all or covetousness not even let be named among you,
As also is proper to saints,

5:4
And filthiness and foolish talking or crude joking which not are fitting,
But rather thanksgiving.

5:5
For this you know
Realizing that any fornicator or unclean person or covetous man,
Who is an idolater,
Not has inheritance in the kingdom of Christ and of God.

5:6
No one you let deceive with empty words,
Because of these things for comes the wrath of God upon the sons of disobedience.

5:7
Not therefore be partakers with them;

5:8
You were for once darkness,
Now however light in the Lord;
As children of light walk,

5:9
For the fruit of the light is in all goodness and righteousness and truth,

5:10
Discerning what is well-pleasing to the Lord;

5:11
And not have fellowship with the works unfruitful of darkness,
Rather however even expose them,

5:12
The things for in secret being done by them shameful it is even to mention;

5:13
But everything being exposed by the light is made visible,

5:14
Everything for becoming visible light is.
Therefore it says;
Awake you,
The one sleeping,
And rise up out from the dead,
And will shine upon you Christ.

5:15
Take heed therefore carefully how you walk,
Not as unwise but as wise,

5:16
Redeeming the time,
Because the days evil are.

5:17
Because of this not be foolish,
But understand what the will of the Lord is;

5:18
And not to be drunk with wine,
In which is debauchery,
Instead be filled with the Spirit,

5:19
Speaking to each other psalms and hymns and songs spiritual,
Singing and making melody in the heart of you to the Lord,

5:20
Giving thanks at all times for all things in the name our Lord Jesus Christ to God and Father,

5:21
Be submitting yourselves to one another in reverence of Christ.

5:22
Wives
To the own husbands as to the Lord,

5:23
For the husband is head of the wife as also Christ is the head of the church,
He Himself Savior of the body.

5:24
But even as the church is subjected to Christ,
So also wives to the own husbands in everything.

5:25
Husbands,
Love the wives just as also Christ loved the church
And Himself gave up for her,

5:26
So that her He might sanctify having cleansed her by the washing of water by the word,

5:27
So that might present He to Himself in glory the church not having spot or wrinkle or any of the such things,
But that it would be holy and blameless.

5:28
So ought also husbands to love the of themselves wives
As the of themselves bodies;
The one loving the of himself wife himself loves,

5:29
No one for at any time the of himself flesh hated,
But he nourishes and cherishes it,
Just as also does Christ the church,

5:30
For members we are of the body his.

5:31
Because of this will leave a man his father and mother
And be joined to the wife of him,
And will be the two into flesh one.

5:32
The mystery this great is,
But i speak as to Christ and as to the church.

5:33
However also you according to individual each
The of himself wife so let love as himself and the wife,
That she may respect the husband.
