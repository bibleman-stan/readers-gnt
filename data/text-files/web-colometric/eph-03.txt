3:1
Of this for reason I Paul the prisoner of Christ Jesus for you

3:2
If indeed you have heard of the administration of the grace of God having been given to me toward you;

3:3
By revelation He made known to me the mystery,
Just as I have written before in brief,

3:4
By which you are able reading it to understand my insight into the mystery of Christ,

3:5
Which in other generations not was made known to the sons of men as now it has been revealed to holy apostles His
And prophets in the Spirit,

3:6
Are the Gentiles joint-heirs and a joint-body and joint-partakers of the promise
In Christ Jesus through the gospel,

3:7
Of which I became servant according to the gift of the grace of God having been given to me according to the working of the power

3:8
To me the very least of all saints was given the grace to the Gentiles to preach the unsearchable riches of Christ,

3:9
And to enlighten everyone what is the administration of the mystery having been hidden from the ages
In God the one all things having created,

3:10
So that should be made known now to the rulers and the authorities
In the heavenly realms through the church the manifold wisdom of God,

3:11
According to the purpose of the ages
Which He accomplished in Christ Jesus our Lord,

3:12
In whom we have boldness and access in confidence by the faith from Him.

3:13
Therefore I implore you not to lose heart at my tribulations for you,
Which is glory of you.

3:14
... of this for reason I bow my knees to the Father,

3:15
From whom every family in the heavens and on earth is named,

3:16
That He might give you according to the riches of his glory with power to be strengthened by his Spirit
In the inner man,

3:17
For to dwell Christ through faith in the hearts of you
In love;
Being rooted and being founded,

3:18
So that you may be fully able to comprehend with all the saints what is the breadth and length and height and depth,

3:19
To know then the surpassing knowledge love of Christ,
So that you may be filled unto all the fullness of God.

3:20
To the one now being able above all things to do exceedingly
That we ask or think
According to the power working in us,

3:21
To Him be the glory in the church and in Christ Jesus
To all the generations of the age of the ages Amen.
