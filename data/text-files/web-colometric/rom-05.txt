5:1
Having been justified therefore by faith peace have with God through the Lord of us Jesus Christ

5:2
through whom also access we have by the faith into the grace this
in which we stand
and we boast in hope of the glory of God

5:3
Not only so now but also we glory in tribulations
knowing that tribulation perseverance produces

5:4
And perseverance character and character hope

5:5
And hope not does make us ashamed
because the love of God has been poured out into the hearts of us
through the Holy Spirit the One having been given to us

5:6
Yet indeed Christ being of us without strength still
according to the right time for the ungodly died

5:7
Rarely indeed for a righteous man anyone will die
on behalf of though the good man
perhaps someone even would dare to die

5:8
Demonstrates however the of Himself love to us God that still sinners being of us
Christ for us died

5:9
Much therefore more having been justified now
by the blood of him we will be saved by Him from the wrath

5:10
If for enemies being we were reconciled to God through the death of the Son of him
much more having been reconciled shall we be saved in the life of him

5:11
Not only so now but also we are rejoicing in God through the Lord of us Jesus Christ
through whom now the reconciliation we have received

5:12
Because of this just as through one man sin into the world entered
and through sin death also thus to all men death passed
for that all sinned

5:13
Until for the law sin was in the world
sin however not is imputed
not there being law

5:14
Nevertheless reigned death from Adam
until Moses even over those not having sinned in the likeness of the transgression of Adam
who is a type of the coming One

5:15
But is not like the trespass so also the gift
If for by the of the one trespass the many died
how much more the grace of God
and the gift in grace which is of the one man Jesus Christ
to the many did abound

5:16
And is not as through one having sinned the gift
The truly for judgment was of one
was unto condemnation but the gift is out of many trespasses unto justification

5:17
If for by the of the one trespass death reigned through the one
how much more those the abundance of grace
and of the gift of righteousness receiving
in life will reign through the one Jesus Christ

5:18
to all men it is unto justification of life
So then just as by one trespass to all men it is unto condemnation so
also by one act of righteousness

5:19
For as for through the disobedience of the one man sinners were made the many
so also through the obedience of the One righteous will be made the many

5:20
The law now entered
so that might abound the trespass
where however abounded sin
overabounded grace

5:21
so that just as reigned the sin in death
so also grace might reign through righteousness unto eternal life
through Jesus Christ the Lord of us
