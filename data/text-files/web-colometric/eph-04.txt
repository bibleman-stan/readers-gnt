4:17
This therefore I say
And testify in the Lord No longer are you to walk as also the Gentiles are walking
In the futility of the mind of them,

4:18
Being darkened in the understanding being alienated from the life of God because of the ignorance
Being in them on account of the hardness of the heart of them,

4:19
Who having cast off all feeling themselves have given up to sensuality
For the working of impurity all with greediness.

4:20
But you not this way learned Christ,

4:21
If indeed Him you have heard
And in Him have been taught just as is the truth in Jesus,

4:22
Are to have put off you concerning the former way of life the old man which is being corrupted according to its desires of deceit,

4:23
To be renewed then in the spirit of the mind of you,

4:24
And to have put on the new man according to God having been created
In righteousness and holiness of truth.

4:25
Therefore having put off falsehood
Let speak truth each one with the neighbor of him,
Because we are of one another members.

4:26
Be angry and yet not sin The sun
Not let set upon anger of you,

4:27
Neither give opportunity to the devil.

4:28
The one stealing no longer let him steal,
Rather however let him toil
Working with the own hands what is good,
So that he may have something to impart to the one need having.

4:29
Any word unwholesome out of the mouth of you not let go forth,
But if any good for edification of the need,
So that it may give grace to those hearing.

4:30
And not grieve the Spirit Holy of God,
In whom you were sealed for the day of redemption.

4:31
All bitterness and rage and anger and clamor and slander let be removed
From you along with all malice.

4:32
Be to one another kind tender-hearted,
Forgiving each other,
As also God in Christ forgave you.
