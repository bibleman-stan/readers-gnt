5:1
It is actually reported that there is sexual immorality among you, and such sexual immorality
as does not even exist among the nations,
that someone has his father's wife.

5:2
And you are puffed up,
and did you not rather mourn,
so that the one who did this deed might be removed from your midst?

5:3
For I indeed, being absent in body
but present in spirit, have already judged as though present
the one who has so produced this,

5:4
in the name of our Lord Jesus, when you are assembled together with my spirit,
with the power of our Lord Jesus,

5:5
to deliver such a one to Satan for the destruction of the flesh,
so that his spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord.

5:6
Your boasting is not good.
Do you not know that a little leaven leavens the whole
lump?

5:7
Clean out the old leaven,
so that you may be a new lump, just as you are unleavened.
For indeed Christ our Passover has been sacrificed;

5:8
therefore let us celebrate the feast, not with old leaven nor with the leaven of malice and wickedness,
but with the unleavened bread of sincerity and truth.

5:9
I wrote to you in my letter not to associate with sexually immoral people,

5:10
not at all meaning the sexually immoral of this world
or the greedy and swindlers or idolaters,
since then you would need to go out of the world.

5:11
But now I have written to you not to associate with anyone called a brother
who is sexually immoral or greedy or an idolater or a reviler or a drunkard or a swindler,
with such a one not even to eat.

5:12
For what have I to do
with judging outsiders?
Is it not
those inside whom you judge,

5:13
while God judges those outside?
Remove the evil person from among yourselves.
