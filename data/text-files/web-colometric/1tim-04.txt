4:1
But the Spirit expressly states
That in later times will depart from some the faith,
Giving heed to spirits deceitful and teachings of demons

4:2
In hypocrisy of speakers of lies having been seared in the own conscience,

4:3
Forbidding to marry commanding to abstain from foods
That God created for reception with thanksgiving by the faithful
And those knowing the truth.

4:4
For every creature of God is good,
And nothing is to be rejected
With thanksgiving being received,

4:5
It is sanctified for by the word of God and prayer.

4:6
These things laying before the brothers
Good you will be a servant of Christ Jesus,
Being nourished in the words of the faith and of the good teaching that you have closely followed,

4:7
But profane and silly fables refuse.
Train rather yourself
To godliness;

4:8
For bodily exercise of a little is profit,
But godliness for everything profitable is,
The promise holding of life of the present and of the one coming.

4:9
Trustworthy is the saying and of full acceptance worthy,

4:10
For this for we toil and strive,
Because we have hope on God the living,
Who is the Savior of all men especially of believers.

4:11
Command these things and teach.

4:12
No one your youth let despise,
But a pattern be for the believers
In speech in conduct in love in faith in purity.

4:13
Until I come give heed to the public reading of Scripture to exhortation to teaching.

4:14
Not be negligent of the in you gift,
Which was given to you through prophecy with the laying on of the hands of the elderhood.

4:15
These things ponder in them be absorbed,
So that your progress evident may be to all;

4:16
Give heed to yourself and to the teaching;
Continue in them;
This for doing
Both yourself you will save and those hearing you.
