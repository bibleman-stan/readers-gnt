1:1
Paul a prisoner of Christ Jesus and Timothy our brother
To Philemon the beloved and our fellow worker

1:2
And to Apphia our sister and to Archippus our fellow soldier
And to the at the house of you church;

1:3
Grace to you and peace from our God Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.

1:4
I thank my God always
Mention of you making upon my prayers,

1:5
Hearing of your love and the faith
That you have toward the Lord Jesus and toward all the saints,

1:6
So that the fellowship of the faith of you effective might become
In the acknowledgment of every good thing that is in us in Christ;

1:7
For joy great I have and encouragement by occasion of the love of you,
Because the hearts of the saints have been refreshed by you,
Brother.

1:8
Therefore much in Christ boldness having to order you what is befitting,

1:9
For the sake of love rather I exhort you,
Such a one being as Paul aged now then also a prisoner of Christ

1:10
I exhort you for my child,
Whom I have begotten in chains Onesimus,

1:11
Once to you useless now however to you and to me useful,

1:12
Whom I have sent back to you in person he who is my very heart;

1:13
Whom I was wishing with myself to keep,
So that on behalf of you me he might serve in the chains of the gospel,

1:14
Apart from however your consent nothing I wished to do,
So that not as according to necessity the good of you may be
But according to willingness;

1:15
For perhaps because of this he was separated from you for a time,
So that eternally him you might possess,

1:16
No longer as a slave but above a slave a brother beloved,
Especially to me how much however more to you both in the flesh and in the Lord.

1:17
If therefore me you consider a partner,
Receive him as me.

1:18
If however in any way he has wronged you or he owes you,
This to me charge;

1:19
I Paul did write with my own hand I will repay it that not I may say to you that even yourself to me you owe also.

1:20
Yes,
Brother,
I from you may have profit in the Lord;
Refresh my heart in Christ.

1:21
Being persuaded of the obedience of you I write to you knowing
That even above what I say you will do.

1:22
At the same time now also prepare for me a lodging,
I hope for that through the prayers of you I will be granted to you.

1:23
Greets you Epaphras fellow prisoner my in Christ Jesus,

1:24
As do Mark Aristarchus Demas and Luke the fellow workers my.

1:25
The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be with the spirit of you.
