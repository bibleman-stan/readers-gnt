4:1
Because of this having the ministry this as we received mercy
not we lose heart

4:2
But we have renounced the hidden things of shame not walking in craftiness
nor falsifying the word of God
but by manifestation of the truth commending ourselves
to every conscience of men before God

4:3
If however indeed is concealed the gospel of us
to those who are perishing it is concealed

4:4
in whom the god of the age this has blinded the minds of the unbelieving
so as for not to beam forth the illumination of the gospel of the glory of Christ
who is the image of God

4:5
Not for ourselves do we proclaim
but Christ Jesus Lord ourselves now servants of you for the sake of Jesus

4:6
For it is God having said
Out of darkness light shall shine
who shone in the hearts of us for the radiance of the knowledge of the glory of God in the face Christ

4:7
We have now the treasure this in earthen vessels
that the surpassingness of the power may be from God
and not from us

4:8
in every way being hard pressed but not being crushed being perplexed but not despairing

4:9
being persecuted but not being forsaken being struck down but not being destroyed

4:10
always the death of Jesus in our body carrying around
so that also the life of Jesus in the body of us should be manifested

4:11
always for we the living to death are being delivered on account of Jesus
so that also the life of Jesus may be manifested in the mortal flesh of us

4:12
So then death in us works
but life in you

4:13
Having however the same spirit of faith
according to that having been written
I believed therefore I have spoken
also we believe therefore also speak

4:14
knowing that the One having raised up the Jesus also us through Jesus will raise up
and will present us with you

4:15
For all things are for the sake of you
so that the grace having abounded through more and more
the thanksgiving may increase to the glory of God

4:16
Therefore not we lose heart
but if indeed the outward of us man is being brought to decay
yet the inner of us is being renewed day and day

4:17
The for momentary lightness affliction our
far surpassing to excessiveness an eternal weight of glory is producing for us

4:18
not are looking at we the things being seen
but the things not being seen
The things for being seen are temporary the things however not being seen are eternal
