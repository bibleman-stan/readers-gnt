3:1
Are we beginning again ourselves to commend
Or not we need like some commendatory letters to you or from you

3:2
The letter of us you are
having been inscribed in the hearts of us
being known and being read by all men

3:3
being revealed that you are a letter of Christ
having been ministered to by us
having been inscribed not in ink but with the Spirit of God the living
not on tablets of stone but on tablets of hearts human

3:4
Confidence now such we have through Christ toward God

3:5
Not that from ourselves sufficient we are
to reckon anything as of ourselves
but the sufficiency of us is of God

3:6
who also has made competent us as ministers of a new covenant not of the letter but of the Spirit
the for letter kills
but the Spirit gives life

3:7
If now the ministry of death in letters having been engraved on stones was produced in glory
so as for not to be able to look intently the sons of Israel into the face of Moses
on account of the glory of the face of him which is fading

3:8
how not more the ministry of the Spirit will be in glory

3:9
If for the ministry of condemnation was glory
much more abounds in the ministry of righteousness in glory

3:10
Even for not has been glorified that having been made glorious
in this respect on account of the surpassing it glory

3:11
If for that which is fading away was through glory
much more is that remaining in glory

3:12
Having therefore such hope
great boldness we use

3:13
and not as Moses would put a veil over the face of himself
for not to look intently the sons of Israel into the end of that fading away

3:14
But were hardened the minds of them
Until for the present day the same veil
at the reading of the old covenant remains
not being lifted which in Christ is being removed

3:15
But unto this day when shall be read Moses
a veil over the heart of them lies

3:16
Whenever however if one shall have turned to the Lord is taken away the veil

3:17
The now Lord the Spirit is
where now the Spirit of the Lord is there is freedom

3:18
We now all having been unveiled in face the glory of the Lord beholding as in a mirror
the same image are being transformed into from glory to glory even as
from the Lord the Spirit
