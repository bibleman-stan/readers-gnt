2:1
And you being dead in the trespasses and the sins of you,

2:2
In which once you walked according to the age the world of this,
According to the ruler of the authority of the air the spirit now working
In the sons of disobedience;

2:3
Among whom also we all lived once in the desires of our flesh,
Doing the things willed of the flesh and of its thoughts,
And we were children by nature of wrath as even the rest;

2:4
But God rich being in mercy because of the great his love
With which He loved us,

2:5
Even being we dead in trespasses made us alive with
By grace you are

2:6
And He raised us up together and seated us together in the heavenly realms in Christ Jesus,

2:7
In order that He might show in the ages that are coming the surpassing riches of his grace
In kindness toward us in Christ Jesus.

2:8
For by grace you are saved through faith;
And this not of yourselves it is of God the gift;

2:9
Not as a result of works so that not anyone may boast.

2:10
Of Him for we are workmanship,
Having been created in Christ Jesus for works good
Which prepared beforehand God,
That in them we should walk.

2:11
Therefore remember that formerly you the Gentiles in the flesh,
The ones being called the uncircumcision by that being called the circumcision in the flesh made by hands,

2:12
That you were at the time that separate from Christ,
Alienated from the commonwealth of Israel and strangers to the covenants of the promise,
Hope not having
And without God in the world.

2:13
But now in Christ Jesus
You the ones once being far off have become near by the blood of Christ.

2:14
He Himself for is our peace having made both one
And the barrier of the fence having broken down the hostility in his flesh,

2:15
The law of commandments in ordinances having annulled so that the two He might create
In Himself into one new man making peace,

2:16
And He might reconcile both in one body to God through the cross
Having slain the hostility by it;

2:17
And having come He proclaimed the gospel peace to you the ones far off
And peace to those near;

2:18
For through Him we have the access both
By one Spirit to the Father.

2:19
So then no longer are you strangers and aliens,
But are fellow citizens of the saints and of the household of God,

2:20
Having been built upon the foundation of the apostles and prophets,
Being the cornerstone Himself Christ Jesus,

2:21
In whom the whole building being fitted together is increasing into a temple holy in the Lord,

2:22
In whom also you are being built together for a habitation of God in the Spirit.
