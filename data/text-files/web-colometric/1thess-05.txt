5:1
Concerning now the times and the seasons,
Brothers,
No need you have to you to be written,

5:2
For yourselves fully you know that the day of the Lord as a thief by night in this manner comes.

5:3
For when they might say;
Peace and security then suddenly upon them comes destruction,
As the labor pains to her in womb having and no not shall they escape.

5:4
You however,
Brothers,
Not are in darkness,
That the day you like a thief should overtake,

5:5
For all you sons of light are and sons of day.
Not we are of night nor of darkness;

5:6
So then not we should sleep as the others but we should watch and we should be sober.

5:7
For those sleeping by night sleep,
And those becoming drunk by night get drunk;

5:8
But we of the day being should be sober,
Having put on the breastplate of faith and love and the helmet the hope of salvation;

5:9
Because not has destined us God for wrath
But for obtaining salvation our through Lord Jesus Christ,

5:10
Who died for us
So that whether we might watch or we might sleep
Together with Him we may live.

5:11
Therefore encourage one another
And build up one another,
Just as also you are doing.

5:12
We implore however you,
Brothers,
To appreciate those toiling among you
And taking the lead over you in the Lord
And admonishing you,

5:13
And to esteem them very highly in love because of the work of them Be at peace among yourselves.

5:14
We exhort now you,
Brothers,
To admonish the unruly,
Encourage the fainthearted,
Help the weak,
Be patient toward all.

5:15
See that no one evil for evil to anyone has repaid,
But always the good pursue toward one another and toward all.

5:16
Always rejoice,

5:17
Unceasingly pray,

5:18
In everything give thanks;
This for is the will of God
In Christ Jesus toward you.

5:19
The Spirit not do quench,

5:20
Prophecies not do despise;

5:21
All things however test,
To the good hold fast,

5:22
From every form of evil abstain.

5:23
Himself now God of peace may sanctify you completely,
And your entirely spirit and soul
And body blameless at the coming our of Lord Jesus Christ may be preserved.

5:24
Is faithful the one calling you,
Who also will do it.

5:25
Brothers,
Pray for us.

5:26
Greet the brothers all with a kiss holy.

5:27
I adjure you by the Lord to be read this letter to all the brothers.

5:28
The grace our of Lord Jesus Christ be with you.
