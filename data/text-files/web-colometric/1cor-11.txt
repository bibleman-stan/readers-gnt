11:1
Imitators of me be
as I also am of Christ

11:2
I commend now you that in all things me you have remembered
and as I delivered to you
the teachings you are keeping

11:3
I want however you to know that of every man the head Christ is
the head now of the woman is the man the head now of Christ God

11:4
Every man praying or prophesying
anything on his head having dishonors the head of him

11:5
Every now woman praying or prophesying
uncovered with the head dishonors the head of her
one for it is and the same with having been shaven

11:6
If for not covers her head a woman
also let her be shorn
If it is now disgraceful to a woman to be shorn or to be shaven let her cover her head

11:7
A man truly for not ought to cover the head
image and glory of God being
the woman however the glory of man is

11:8
Not for is man of woman
but woman of man

11:9
Truly for not was created man on account of the woman
but woman on account of the man

11:10
Because of this ought the woman authority to have on the head on account of the angels

11:11
However neither is woman separate from man nor man separate from woman in the Lord

11:12
Just as for the woman of the man is so also the man by the woman is
the however all things of God

11:13
In you yourselves judge
Becoming is it for a woman revealed to God to pray

11:14
Not even nature itself does teach you that a man indeed if he has long hair
a dishonor to him it is

11:15
a woman however if she has long hair
glory to her it is
For the long hair instead of a covering is given

11:16
If now anyone is inclined contentious to be
we such custom no other have
nor the churches of God

11:17
In this now instructing
not I do praise because not for the better but for the worse you come together

11:18
First indeed for coming together you in an assembly
I hear divisions among you there to be
and in part it I believe

11:19
It behooves for also factions among you there to be
so that also the approved evident should become among you

11:20
Coming therefore of you together in one place
not it is the Lord’s supper to eat

11:21
One for the own supper takes first in eating
and this one indeed is hungry
that one however is drunken

11:22
No indeed houses not have you in which to eat and to drink
Or the church of God do you despise
and put to shame those nothing having
What shall I say to you
Shall I praise you in this
Not I praise you

11:23
I for received from the Lord
that which also I delivered to you
that the Lord Jesus in the night
in which He was betrayed took bread

11:24
and having given thanks He broke it
and said
This of me is the body which is for you
this do in of me remembrance

11:25
Likewise also the cup after having supped
saying
This cup the new covenant is in My blood
this do as often as if you might drink it
in of me remembrance

11:26
As often as for if you may eat the bread this
and the cup may drink
the death of the Lord you proclaim until that He should come

11:27
Therefore whoever should eat the bread
or should drink the cup of the Lord
unworthily guilty will be of the body and of the blood of the Lord

11:28
Let examine however a man himself
and in this manner of the bread let him eat
and of the cup let him drink

11:29
The one for eating and drinking judgment on himself eats and drinks
not discerning the body

11:30
Because of this among you many are weak and sick and are fallen asleep many

11:31
If for ourselves we were judging
not we would come under judgment

11:32
Being judged however by Lord we are disciplined
so that not with the world we should be condemned

11:33
So then
brothers of me
coming together in order to eat
one another wait for

11:34
If anyone is hungry
at home let him eat
so that not for judgment you might come together
And the other things as soon as I might come I will set in order
