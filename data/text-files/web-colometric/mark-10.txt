10:1
And from there having risen up He comes into the region of Judea and beyond the Jordan
And come together again crowds to him
and as He had been accustomed again He was teaching them

10:2
And were demanding of him if it is lawful for a husband
a wife to divorce
testing Him

10:3
And He answering said to them
What you did command Moses

10:4
And they said
Permitted Moses a roll of divorce to write and to send her away

10:5
But Jesus said to them
Because of the hardness of heart of you He wrote for you the commandment this

10:6
From however the beginning of creation Male and female He made them

10:7
On account of this will leave a man the father of him and mother
and joined to the wife his

10:8
and will be the two for flesh one
Therefore no longer they are
two but one flesh

10:9
What therefore God has joined together
man not let separate

10:10
And in the house again the disciples concerning this were asking Him

10:11
And He says to them
Whoever shall divorce the wife of him
and shall marry another commits adultery against her

10:12
And if a woman having divorced the husband of her
should marry another she commits adultery

10:13
And they were bringing to him little children
that them He might touch
But the disciples rebuked them

10:14
Having seen now Jesus was indignant
and said to them
Permit the little children to come to me
not do hinder them
To the for such belongs the kingdom of God

10:15
Truly I say to you
whoever not shall receive the kingdom of God as a child no not shall enter into it

10:16
And having taken them in arms them He was blessing
having laid the hands on them

10:17
And going forth of him on the journey
having run up one and having knelt down to him
was asking Him
Teacher good what shall I do
that life eternal I might inherit

10:18
And Jesus said to him
Why Me call you good
No one is good if not alone God

10:19
The commandments you know
Not shall you murder
not shall you commit adultery
not shall you steal
not shall you bear false witness
not shall you defraud
you shall honor the father of you and the mother

10:20
And he was saying to him
Teacher these all I have kept from the youth of me

10:21
And Jesus having looked upon him loved him
and said to him
One thing to you is lacking
Go
as much as you have
sell and give to the poor
and you will have treasure in heaven
and come follow Me

10:22
And having been sad at the word he went away grieving he was one
for having possessions many

10:23
And having looked around Jesus says the disciples of him
How difficultly those riches having
into the kingdom of God will enter

10:24
And the disciples were astonished at the words of him
But Jesus again answering says to them
Children how difficult it is
into the kingdom of God to enter

10:25
Easier it is
for a camel through the eye of the needle to pass
than for a rich man into the kingdom of God to enter

10:26
And exceedingly they were astonished saying among themselves
Then who is able to be saved

10:27
Having looked on them
Jesus says
With men it is impossible
but not with God all things for are possible with God

10:28
Began to say Peter to him
Behold we have left all
and followed You

10:29
Was saying Jesus
Truly I say to you
no one there is who has left house or brothers or sisters or mother or father or children or lands
for the sake of me and because of the gospel

10:30
if not shall take a hundredfold now
in the time this houses and brothers
and sisters and mothers and children and lands with persecutions
and in the age which is coming life eternal

10:31
Many however will be first last
and the last first

10:32
They were then on the way going up to Jerusalem and was going on before them Jesus
and they were astonished
those then following were afraid
And having taken to him again the Twelve
He began them to tell the things being about to him to happen

10:33
Behold we go up to Jerusalem
and the Son of Man will be betrayed to the chief priests and to the scribes
and they will condemn Him to death
and will betray Him to the Gentiles

10:34
And they will mock Him
and will spit upon him and will flog Him and will kill
and on the third day He will rise again

10:35
And come up to him James and John the sons of Zebedee saying to him
Teacher we desire
that whatever if we might ask you You would do for us

10:36
And He said to them
What do you desire to do for you

10:37
And they said to him
Grant to us that one of us at Your right hand and one at Your left hand we might sit in the glory of You

10:38
And Jesus said to them
Not you know what you ask
Are you able to drink the cup
which I drink and the baptism
which I am baptized to be baptized with

10:39
And they said to him
We are able
And Jesus said to them
The cup which I drink
you will drink and the baptism
which I am baptized you will be baptized with

10:40
but to sit at the right hand of me or at the left hand not is
Mine to give
but to those for whom it has been prepared

10:41
And having heard this the ten began to be indignant about James and John

10:42
And having called near them
Jesus says to them
You know that those being accounted to rule over the Gentiles exercise lordship over them
and those great ones of them exercise authority over them

10:43
Not thus however shall it be among you
Instead whoever desires great to become among you will be your servant

10:44
and whoever desires among you to become first will be of all slave

10:45
Even for the Son of Man not came to be served
but to serve
and to give the life of him as a ransom for many

10:46
And they come to Jericho
And as was going out He from Jericho
and the disciples of him and a crowd large
the son of Timaeus Bartimaeus blind beggar was sitting beside the road

10:47
And having heard that Jesus of Nazareth it is
he began to cry out and to say
Son of David Jesus have mercy on me

10:48
And were rebuking him many that he should be silent
but much more he kept crying out
Son of David have mercy on me

10:49
And having stopped Jesus commanded
to be called him
And they call the blind man saying to him
Take courage
Rise up He calls you

10:50
And having cast away the cloak of him
having risen up he came to Jesus

10:51
And answering him Jesus says
What to you do you desire I should do
And the blind man said to him
Rabboni that I may receive sight

10:52
And Jesus said to him
Go
the faith of you has healed you
And immediately he received sight
and he began following Him on the way
