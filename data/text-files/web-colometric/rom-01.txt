1:1
Paul a servant of Christ Jesus a called apostle having been set apart for the gospel of God

1:2
which He promised beforehand through the prophets of him in the Scriptures Holy

1:3
concerning the Son of him having come of the seed of David according to flesh

1:4
having been declared Son of God in power according to the Spirit of holiness by resurrection from the dead
Jesus Christ the Lord of us

1:5
through whom we have received grace and apostleship unto obedience of faith
among all the Gentiles on behalf of the name of him

1:6
among whom are also you called of Jesus Christ

1:7
To all those being in Rome beloved of God called saints
Grace to you and peace
from God the Father of us and the Lord Jesus Christ

1:8
First indeed I thank the God of me through Jesus Christ for all of you
because the faith of you is being proclaimed in all the world

1:9
Witness for my is God
whom I serve in the spirit of me in the gospel of the Son of him
how unceasingly mention of you I make

1:10
always upon the prayers of me imploring if perhaps now at last I will make a prosperous journey
by the will of God to come to you

1:11
I long for to see you
that some I may impart gift to you spiritual to the strengthening of you

1:12
that now is to be encouraged together among you
through the among one another faith of you both and of me

1:13
Not I do want now you to be ignorant
brothers
that many times I purposed to come to you
and was hindered until the present
that some fruit I might have also among you
as even among the other Gentiles

1:14
To Greeks both and to barbarians to the wise both and the foolish a debtor I am

1:15
Thus as to me there is readiness also to you who are in Rome to preach the gospel

1:16
Not for I am ashamed of the gospel
the power for of God it is unto salvation to everyone believing
to Jewish both first and to Greek

1:17
The righteousness for of God in it is revealed from faith to faith as it has been written
And the righteous by faith will live

1:18
Is revealed for the wrath of God from heaven upon all ungodliness and unrighteousness
of men the truth by unrighteousness suppressing

1:19
because the known of God manifest is among them God
for to them has revealed it

1:20
The for invisible qualities of him from the creation of the world by the things made being understood are clearly seen
the both eternal of him power and divinity for to be them without excuse

1:21
For having known God
not as God they glorified Him or were thankful
but they became futile in the thinking of them
and was darkened the foolish of them heart

1:22
Professing to be wise they became fools

1:23
and quadrupeds and creeping things
and they changed the glory of the immortal God
into a likeness of an image of mortal man and birds

1:24
Therefore gave up them God in the desires of the hearts of them
to impurity to dishonor the bodies of them between themselves

1:25
who changed the truth of God into the falsehood
and reverenced and served the created thing beyond the One having created it
who is blessed to the ages Amen

1:26
Because of this gave up them God to passions of dishonor
Even for females of them changed the natural use
into that contrary to nature

1:27
Likewise then also the males having left the natural use of the female
were inflamed in the desire of them toward one another
males with males the shame working out
and the recompense which was fitting of the error of them in themselves receiving

1:28
And as not they did see fit God to have in their knowledge
gave up them God to a depraved mind to do things not being proper

1:29
being filled with all unrighteousness wickedness covetousness malice
full of envy murder strife deceit maliciousness gossips

1:30
slanderers hateful to God insolent arrogant boastful inventors
of evil things to parents disobedient

1:31
foolish untrustworthy heartless unmerciful

1:32
who the righteous decree of God having known
that those such things doing
worthy of death are
not only them are practicing
but also are approving of those practicing them
