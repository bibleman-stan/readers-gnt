5:1
In freedom us Christ has set free stand firm
Therefore and not again in a yoke of slavery entangle yourselves.

5:2
Behold I Paul say to you that if you shall become circumcised
Christ you nothing will profit.

5:3
I testify now again to every man being circumcised that a debtor he is all the Law to keep.

5:4
You are severed from Christ
Whoever in the Law are being justified,
From grace you have fallen away.

5:5
For we through the Spirit by faith the hope of righteousness eagerly await.

5:6
For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision any has power nor uncircumcision,
But only faith through love working.

5:7
You were running well;
Who you cut into the truth not to obey?

5:8
This persuasion is not of the one calling you.

5:9
A little leaven whole the lump leavens.

5:10
I am confident as to you in the Lord that no other mind will you have;
The one however troubling
You will bear the judgment whoever if he might be.

5:11
I now,
Brothers,
If circumcision still proclaim,
Why still am I persecuted?
In that case has been abolished the offense of the cross.

5:12
I wish also will emasculate themselves those upsetting you.

5:13
For you to freedom were called,
Brothers;
But not the freedom for an opportunity to the flesh
Rather through love serve one another;

5:14
For the entire Law in one word is fulfilled,
In this You shall love the neighbor of you as yourself.

5:15
If however one another you bite and devour take heed
Lest by one another you might be consumed.

5:16
I say now by the Spirit walk and the desire of the flesh no not you should gratify.

5:17
For the flesh desires against the Spirit,
And the Spirit against the flesh,
These for to one another are opposed
In order not that if you might wish those things you should do.

5:18
If however the Spirit you are led by,
Not you are under the Law.

5:19
Evident now are the works of the flesh,
Which are sexual immorality impurity sensuality,

5:20
Idolatry sorcery enmities strife jealousy outbursts of anger contentions dissensions factions,

5:21
Envyings drunkennesses carousing and things like these,
As to which I forewarn you,
Even as I warned before that those such things doing kingdom God’s not will inherit.

5:22
But the fruit of the Spirit is love joy peace patience kindness goodness faithfulness,

5:23
Gentleness self-control;
Against things such no there is law.

5:24
Those now of Christ the flesh have crucified with its passions and desires.

5:25
If we live by the Spirit,
By the Spirit also we should walk.

5:26
Not we should become boastful,
One another provoking,
One another envying.
