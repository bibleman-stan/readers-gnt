4:1
And again He began to teach beside the sea
And was gathered together to him a crowd great
so that He into a boat having entered sat in the sea
and all the crowd close to the sea on the land was

4:2
And He began teaching them in parables many things
and He was saying to them in the teaching of him

4:3
Listen
Behold went out the one sowing to sow

4:4
And it came to pass as he sowed
some fell along the road
and came the birds
and devoured it

4:5
And other fell upon the rocky place where not it had soil much
and immediately it sprang up because of the not having depth of soil

4:6
And after rose the sun
it was scorched
and because of not having root it withered away

4:7
And other fell among the thorns
and grew up the thorns
and choked it
and fruit no it yielded

4:8
And other fell into the soil good
and began yielding fruit growing up and increasing
and bearing one thirtyfold
and one sixty
and one a hundred

4:9
And He was saying
He who has ears to hear let him hear

4:10
And when He was with alone
began asking Him those around Him with the Twelve about the parable

4:11
And He was saying to them
To you the mystery has been given of the kingdom of God
To those however who are outside in parables everything is done

4:12
so that Seeing they might see and not perceive
and hearing they might hear and not understand
lest they should turn
and should be forgiven them

4:13
And He says to them
Not understand you the parable this
And how all the parables will you understand

4:14
The one sowing the word sows

4:15
These now are they along the road where is sown the word
and when they hear
immediately comes Satan
and takes away the word having been sown in them

4:16
And these are likewise those upon the rocky places sown
who when they hear the word
immediately with joy receive it

4:17
and not they have root in themselves
but temporary are
then having arisen tribulation or persecution on account of the word
immediately they fall away

4:18
And these are those among the thorns sown
These are those the word having heard

4:19
and the cares of this age
and the deceit of the riches
and of the other things desires entering in
choke the word
and unfruitful it becomes

4:20
And these are those upon the soil good having been sown
such as hear the word
and receive it
and bring forth fruit
one thirtyfold
and one sixty
and one a hundred

4:21
And He was saying to them
Not is brought in the lamp
so that under the basket it might be put or under the bed
Is it not that upon the lampstand it might be put

4:22
Nothing for there is hidden if not that it should be made manifest
nor has taken place a secret thing but that it should come to light

4:23
If anyone has ears to hear let him hear

4:24
And He was saying to them
Take heed what you hear
With what measure you measure it will be measured to you
and more will be added to you

4:25
Whoever for may have it will be given to him
and he who not has even that which he has will be taken away from him

4:26
And He was saying
Thus is the kingdom of God
as a man should cast the seed upon the earth

4:27
and should sleep
and rise night and day
and the seed should sprout and grow
how not knows he

4:28
Of itself the earth brings forth fruit
first a plant
then an ear
then full grain in the ear

4:29
When then offers itself the fruit
immediately he sends the sickle
for has come the harvest

4:30
And He was saying
To what shall we liken the kingdom of God
Or in what it parable shall we present

4:31
As to a grain of mustard
which when it has been sown upon the earth
smallest is of all the seeds which are upon the earth

4:32
and when it has been sown
it grows up
and becomes greater than all the garden plants
and it produces branches great
so that are able under the shadow of it the birds of the air to perch

4:33
And with such parables many He kept speaking to them the word
as they were able to hear

4:34
Without then parables not would He speak to them
in private however to His own disciples He would explain all things

4:35
And He says to them on that day evening having come
Let us pass over to the other side

4:36
And having dismissed the crowd
they take with them Him since He was in the boat
also other boats were with him

4:37
And comes a storm violent of wind
And the waves were breaking over the boat
so that already is being filled up the boat

4:38
And He was in the stern
on the cushion sleeping
And they awaken Him
and say to him
Teacher
not is it concern to You that we perish

4:39
And having been awoken He rebuked the wind
and said to the sea
Silence
be still
And abated the wind
and there was a calm great

4:40
And He said to them
Why fearful are you
still ~ no have you faith

4:41
And they feared with fear great
and were saying to each other
Who then this is
that even the wind and the sea obey Him
