16:1
I commend now to you Phoebe the sister of us
being also a servant of the church in Cenchrea

16:2
that her you might receive in the Lord worthily of the saints
and you might assist her
in whatever of you she may need
matter Also for she a patroness of many has been and of me myself

16:3
Greet Prisca and Aquila the fellow workers of me in Christ Jesus

16:4
who for the life of me the of them neck have lain down
whom not I only thank but also all the churches of the Gentiles

16:5
and the at house their church
Greet Epenetus the beloved of me
who is a firstfruit of Asia for Christ

16:6
Greet Mary, who much toiled for you

16:7
Greet Andronicus and Junias kinsmen of me and fellow prisoners with me
who are of note among the apostles
who also before me were in Christ

16:8
Greet Ampliatus beloved of me in the Lord

16:9
Greet Urbanus the fellow worker of us in Christ and Stachys the beloved of me

16:10
Greet Apelles the approved in Christ Greet
those of the household of Aristobulus

16:11
Greet Herodion the kinsman of me Greet
those of the household of Narcissus being in the Lord

16:12
Greet Tryphena and Tryphosa those toiling in the Lord Greet
Persis the beloved
who much toiled in the Lord

16:13
Greet Rufus the chosen in the Lord and the mother of him and of me

16:14
Greet Asyncritus Phlegon Hermes Patrobas Hermas and the with them brothers

16:15
Greet Philologus and Julia Nereus and the sister of him
and Olympas and the with them all saints

16:16
Greet one another with a kiss holy
Greet you the churches all of Christ

16:17
I exhort now you
brothers
to watch out for those divisions and obstacles contrary to the teaching
that you have learned
causing and turn away from them

16:18
For such the Lord of us Christ not serve
but the of themselves belly
and by smooth talk and flattery they deceive the hearts of the naive

16:19
The for of you obedience to all has reached
Over you therefore I rejoice
I wish however you wise to be
to good innocent however to evil

16:20
The now God of peace will crush Satan under the feet of you in a short time
The grace of the Lord of us Jesus Christ be with you

16:21
Greets you Timothy the fellow worker of me
also Lucius and Jason and Sosipater the kinsmen of me

16:22
Greet you I Tertius the one having written down this letter in the Lord

16:23
Greets you Gaius the host of me and of all the church
Greets you Erastus the steward of the city and Quartus the brother

16:24
The grace of the Lord of us Jesus Christ with all of you, amen.
