3:1
If then you have been raised with Christ,
The things above seek,
Where Christ is at the right hand of God sitting;

3:2
The things above your set minds on,
Not the things on the earth,

3:3
You have died for and the life of you has been hidden with Christ in God;

3:4
When Christ may be your revealed life,
Then also you with Him will appear in glory.

3:5
Put to death therefore the members which are upon the earth sexual immorality impurity passion desire evil,
And covetousness
Which is idolatry,

3:6
Because of which things is coming the wrath of God upon the sons the disobedience;

3:7
In which also you walked once,
When you were living in them;

3:8
But now put off also you all these things anger rage malice slander foul language
Out of the mouth of you;

3:9
Not do lie to one another;
Having put off the old man with the practices of him,

3:10
And having put on the new
The one being renewed in knowledge according to the image
Of who created him,

3:11
Where not there is Greek and Jew circumcision and uncircumcision,
Barbarian Scythian slave free,
But all and in all Christ is.

3:12
Put on therefore as the elect of God holy and beloved,
Hearts of compassion kindness humility gentleness and patience,

3:13
Bearing with each other and forgiving each other
If anyone against another should have a complaint;
Even as also the Lord has forgiven you so
Also you;

3:14
Beyond all now these put on the love,
Which is the bond of perfect unity.

3:15
And the peace from Christ let rule in the hearts of you,
To which also you were called in one body;
And thankful be.

3:16
The word of Christ let dwell in you richly
In all wisdom;
Teaching and admonishing each other in psalms hymns and songs spiritual
With grace singing in the hearts of you to God;

3:17
And everything which what if you might do in word or in deed,
All in the name of the Lord Jesus giving thanks to God the Father through Him.

3:18
Wives,
Submit yourselves to the husbands as is fitting in the Lord.

3:19
Husbands,
Love the wives
And not be harsh toward them.

3:20
Children,
Obey the parents in all things,
This for pleasing is in the Lord.

3:21
Fathers,
Not do provoke the children of you,
That not they might become discouraged.

3:22
Slaves,
Obey in all things the according to flesh masters,
Not with eye-services as men-pleasers
But in sincerity of heart fearing the Lord.

3:23
Whatever if you might do,
From the soul work as to the Lord and not to men,

3:24
Knowing that from the Lord you will receive the reward of the inheritance;
The Lord Christ you serve;

3:25
The one for doing wrong will be repaid for what he has done wrong,
And not there is partiality.
