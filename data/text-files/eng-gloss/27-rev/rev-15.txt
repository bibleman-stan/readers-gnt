15:1
And I saw another sign in heaven, great and marvelous:
seven angels having seven plagues, the last ones,
because with them the wrath of God is completed.


15:2
And I saw what appeared to be a sea of glass mingled with fire,
and those who had conquered the beast
and its image
and the number of its name,
standing beside the sea of glass,
holding harps of God.


15:3
And they sing the song of Moses, the servant of God,
and the song of the Lamb, saying:
Great and marvelous are your works, Lord,
God Almighty;
righteous and true are your ways, O King of the ages;


15:4
who will not fear you,
Lord,
and glorify your name?
For you alone are holy;
for all the nations will come and worship before you,
for your righteous acts have been revealed.


15:5
And after these things I looked,
and the temple of the tent of witness in heaven was opened,


15:6
and out came the seven angels having the seven plagues from the temple,
clothed in pure bright linen
and wrapped around their chests with golden sashes.


15:7
And one of the four living creatures gave to the seven angels seven golden bowls
full of the wrath of God who lives forever and ever.


15:8
And the temple was filled with smoke from the glory of God and from his power,
and no one could enter the temple
until the seven plagues of the seven angels were completed.
