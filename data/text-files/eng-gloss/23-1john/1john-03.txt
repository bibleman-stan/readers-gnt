3:1
Behold what love has given to us the Father
that children of God we should be called and we are
Because of this the world not knows us
because not it knew Him

3:2
Beloved,
now children of God are we
and not yet has been revealed what we will be
We know that when He appears like Him we will be
for we will see Him as He is

3:3
And everyone having the hope this in Him purifies himself
just as He pure is

3:4
Everyone committing sin also lawlessness commits
and sin is lawlessness

3:5
And you know that He appeared
so that sins He might take away
and sin in Him not there is

3:6
Anyone in Him abiding not sins
anyone sinning not has seen Him nor has he known Him

3:7
Little children
no one let lead astray you
the one practicing righteousness righteous is
just as He righteous is

3:8
The one practicing sin of the devil is
because from the beginning the devil has been sinning
For this reason was revealed the Son of God
so that He might destroy the works of the devil

3:9
Anyone having been born of God sin not practices because seed of
him in him abides and not he is
able to continue sinning because
of God he has been born

3:10
Through this manifest are the children of God and the children of the devil
Anyone not practicing righteousness not is of God
and also the one not loving the brother of him

3:11
For this is the message that you have heard from the beginning
that we should love one another

3:12
not as Cain who of the evil one was and slew the brother of him
And because of what he slayed him
Because the works of him evil were
those however of the brother of him righteous

3:13
not be surprised
brothers,
if hates you the world

3:14
We know that we have passed from death to life
because we love our brothers
The one not loving abides in death

3:15
Everyone hating the brother of him a murderer is
and you know that any murderer not has eternal life in him abiding

3:16
By this we have known love
because He for us the life of him laid down
and we ought for our brothers our lives to lay down

3:17
Whoever now might have the goods of the world
and might see the brother of him need having
and might close up the heart of him from him
how the love of God abides in him

3:18
Little children
not we should love in word nor in tongue
but in action and in truth

3:19
by this we will know that of the truth we are
and before him we will assure the heart of us

3:20
that if should condemn us our heart
that greater than is God the heart of us and He knows all things

3:21
Beloved,
if the heart not should condemn us our
confidence we have toward God

3:22
and whatever if we might ask we receive from him
because the commandments of him we keep
and the things pleasing before him we do

3:23
And this is the commandment of him
that we should believe in the name the Son of him Jesus Christ
and we should love one another
just as He gave the commandment to us

3:24
And the one keeping the commandments of him in Him abides and He in him
And by this we know that He abides in us
by the Spirit whom to us He has given
