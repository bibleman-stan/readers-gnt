and hypocrisy and envies
and all evil speakings

2:2
like newborn babies reasonable pure milk crave
so that by it you may grow up in respect to salvation

if indeed you have tasted that is good the Lord

2:4
To whom coming a stone living
by men indeed rejected
in the sight of however God chosen and precious

2:5
also you yourselves as stones living are being built up
as a house spiritual into a priesthood holy
to offer spiritual sacrifices acceptable to God through Jesus Christ

2:6
Therefore it is contained in Scripture
Behold I lay in Zion a stone cornerstone a chosen precious
and the one believing on Him no not shall be put to shame

2:7
To you therefore is the preciousness believing to those
disobeying however The stone which have rejected those building
this has become into the head of the corner

2:8
and A stone of stumbling and a rock of offense
They stumble at
to the word being disobedient
to which also they were appointed

2:9
You however are a race chosen
a royal priesthood
a nation holy
a people for His possession
so that the excellencies you may proclaim of the One out of darkness you having called
to the marvelous of him light

2:10
who once were not a people
now however the people of God
those not having received mercy now however having received mercy

2:11
Beloved,
I exhort you as aliens and sojourners
to abstain from fleshly desires
which war against the soul

2:12
the conduct of you among the Gentiles keeping honorable so that
wherein which they speak against you as evildoers through the good deeds
having witnessed they may glorify God in the day of visitation

2:13
Be in subjection to every human institution for the sake of the Lord
whether to the king as being supreme

2:14
or to governors as by him being sent
for vengeance to evildoers praise then to well doers

2:15
because this is the will of God
doing good to put to silence the of foolish men ignorance

as free
and not as a cover-up having for evil the freedom
but as of God servants

2:17
Everyone honor
The brotherhood love
God fear
the king honor

2:18
Servants be subject
with all fear to masters
not only to the good and gentle but also to the unreasonable

2:19
This for is acceptable
if for sake of conscience toward God endures anyone griefs
suffering unjustly

2:20
What kind of for credit is it if sinning and being struck you shall endure
But if doing good and suffering you shall endure
this is commendable before God

2:21
To this hereunto for you have
been called because also Christ suffered for you you
leaving an example that
you should follow after the steps of him

2:22
Who sin not committed
neither was found trickery in the mouth of him

2:23
Who being reviled not reviled in return
suffering not threatened
He gave Himself over however to him judging justly

2:24
Who the sins of us Himself bore
in the body of him on the tree
so that to sins having been dead to righteousness we might live
By whose wounds you have been healed

2:25
You were for like sheep going astray
but you have returned now
