5:1
Elders among you I exhort
a fellow elder and witness of the of Christ sufferings
who am also of the being about to be revealed glory partaker

5:2
Shepherd the among you flock of God
exercising oversight not under compulsion but willingly according to God
and not for base gain but eagerly

5:3
not as exercising lordship over those in your charge
but examples being to the flock

5:4
And having been revealed the Chief Shepherd you will receive the unfading of glory crown

5:5
Likewise
younger ones
be subject to your elders
All now one to another humility gird on
because God the proud opposes
to the humble however He gives grace

5:6
Be humbled therefore under the mighty hand of God
so that you He may exalt in due time

5:7
all the anxiety of you having cast upon him because
with him there is care about you

5:8
Be sober-minded watch
The adversary of You the devil
as a lion roaring prowls about
seeking whom to devour

5:9
whom you should resist firm in the faith
knowing the same sufferings throughout the world in your brotherhood to be accomplished

5:10
The now God of all grace
the one having called you to the eternal of him glory in Christ
a little while of you having suffered He Himself will perfect
will confirm will strengthen and will establish you

5:11
To Him be the power to the ages of the ages Amen

5:12
Through Silvanus to you the faithful brother as I regard him
through few words I have written
exhorting and testifying this to be the true grace of God
in which you stand

5:13
Greets you she in Babylon elected with you and Mark the son of me

5:14
Greet one another with a kiss of love
Peace be to you
all who are in Christ
