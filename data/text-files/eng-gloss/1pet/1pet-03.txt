3:1
Likewise wives be subject to the own husbands so that even if any are disobedient to
the word by the of the wives conduct without word they will be won over

3:2
having witnessed the in respect pure conduct of you

3:3
whose let it be not the external of braiding of hair
and putting around of gold or putting on of garments adorning

3:4
but the hidden of the heart man
in the imperishable of the gentle and quiet spirit
which is before God of great worth

3:5
In this way for formerly also the holy women hoping in God used to adorn themselves
being subject to the own husbands

3:6
as Sarah obeyed Abraham
lord him calling
of whom you have become children doing good and not fearing not one consternation

3:7
Husbands likewise dwelling with them according to knowledge
as with a weaker vessel
with the female rendering honor as also joint-heirs of the grace of life
so as for not to be hindered the prayers of you

3:8
Now the end All be single-minded sympathetic loving as brothers tender-hearted humble

3:9
not repaying evil for evil or insult for insult on the contrary however
blessing because to this you were called so that blessing you should inherit

3:10
The one for desiring life to love and to see days good
let him keep the tongue from evil
and lips not to speak deceit

3:11
Let him turn away also from evil
and let him do good
Let him seek peace
and let him pursue it

3:12
Because the eyes of the Lord are on the righteous
and the ears of him toward prayer of them The face however of the Lord is against those doing evil

3:13
And who is he who will harm you if for that which is good zealous you should be

3:14
But if even you should suffer because of righteousness
you are blessed
And the threats of them not you should be afraid of
neither should you be troubled

3:15
As Lord however Christ sanctify in the hearts of you
ready always for a defense
to everyone asking you an account concerning the in you hope

3:16
yet with gentleness and fear a conscience having good so that in this they might speak
against you they shall be ashamed those reviling your good in Christ manner of life

3:17
It is better for doing good if wills it the will of God to suffer than doing evil

3:18
because also Christ once for sins suffered
the righteous for the unrighteous
so that you He might bring to God having been put to death indeed in the flesh having been made alive however in the spirit

3:19
in which also to the in prison spirits having gone He preached

3:20
having disobeyed at one time when was waiting the of God longsuffering in the days of Noah
being prepared of the ark
in which a few that is eight souls
were saved through water

3:21
through the resurrection of Jesus Christ
which also you prefigures now saving baptism not of flesh a putting away of the filth
but of a conscience good the demand toward God

3:22
who is at the right hand of God
having gone into heaven
having been subjected to him angels and authorities and powers
