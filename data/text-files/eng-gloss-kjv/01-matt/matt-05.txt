and when was sitting down He
they came to Him the disciples of Him.

for theirs is the kingdom of the heavens.

5:4
Blessed [are] those mourning,
for they themselves will be comforted.

5:5
Blessed [are] the meek,
for they themselves will inherit the earth.

5:6
Blessed [are] those hungering and thirsting for the righteousness,

5:7
Blessed [are] the merciful,

Blessed [are] the pure the in heart,

Blessed [are] the peacemakers,

5:10
Blessed [are] those persecuted on account of righteousness,
for theirs is the kingdom of the heavens.

5:11
Blessed are you
when they may insult you and they may persecute [you]
and they may say all kinds of evil declaration against you lying on account of Me.

5:12
do rejoice and do exult,
for the reward of you [is] great in the heavens;
thus for they persecuted the prophets the before you.

5:13
You yourselves are the salt of the earth;
if however the salt shall become tasteless,
with what will it be salted?
For no [thing] it is useful any longer only except for being cast out and is to be trampled upon by the men.

5:14
You yourselves are the light of the world;
Not is able a city to be hidden on a hill lying;

5:15
Nor do they light a lamp
and put it under the a basket but upon the lampstand,
and it shines for all those in the house.

5:16
Thus should shine the light of you before the men
so that they may see your the good works
and they may glorify the Father of you who [is] in the heavens.

5:17
Not may think that I have come to abolish the law or the Prophets;
not I have come to abolish
but to fulfill.

5:18
Amen for I say to you;
until when may pass away the heaven and the earth,
iota one or one stroke of a letter certainly not may pass away from the law
until when everything may happen.

5:19
Who[ever] if then shall break one of the commandments of these the least
and shall teach so the others,
least he will be called in the kingdom of the heavens.
who[ever] now maybe may keep and may teach [them],
this [one] great will be called in the kingdom of the heavens.

5:20
I say for to you
that only unless shall abound your the righteousness above [that] of the scribes and Pharisees,
certainly not shall you enter into the kingdom of the heavens.

5:21
You have heard that it was said to the ancients;
Not you will murder;
who[ever] now maybe may murder,
liable will be to the judgment;’

5:22
I myself however say to you
that everyone who is being angry with the brother of him
in vain liable will be to the judgment;
who[ever] now maybe may say to the brother of him;
Raca,’
liable will be to the Sanhedrin;
who[ever] now maybe may say;
Fool,
liable will be to the hell the of fire.

5:23
If therefore you shall offer the gift of you at the altar
and there shall remember that the brother of you has something against you,

5:24
do leave there the gift of you before the altar
and do go away,
first do be reconciled to the brother of you,
and then having come do offer the gift of you.

5:25
do be agreeing with the accuser of you quickly
before which you are with him on the way
otherwise you shall deliver the accuser to the judge,
and the judge you to the to the officer,
and into prison you will be cast.

5:26
Amen I say to you;
certainly not you may come out from there
until when you may pay the last kodranten!

5:27
You have heard that it was said to the ancients:

5:28
I myself however say to you
that everyone who is looking upon a woman in order the to lust after her
already has committed adultery with her in the heart of him.

5:29
If now the eye of you the right causes to stumble you,
do pluck out it and do cast [it] from you;
it is better indeed for you
that may perish one of the members of you
and not all the body of you may be cast into hell.

5:30
And if the right of you hand causes to stumble you,
do cut off it and do cast [it] from you;
it is better indeed for you
that may perish one of the members of you
and not all the body of you into hell may depart.

5:31
It was said also
that: Who[ever] maybe may divorce the wife of him,
he should give to her a letter of divorce.’

5:32
I myself however say to you
that everyone who is divorcing the wife of him
except on account of sexual immorality causes her to be adulterated;
And who[ever] if divorced shall marry, commits adultery.

5:33
Again you have heard that it was said to the ancients;
Not will you swear falsely,
you will keep now to the Lord the oaths of you.

5:34
I myself however say to you not to swear at all,
neither [swear] by the heaven
because [the] throne it is the of God,

5:35
nor [swear] by the earth
because [the] footstool it is of the feet of Him,
nor [swear] by Jerusalem
because [the] city it is of the great King,

5:36
nor by the head of you may you swear
because not you are able one hair white to make or black.

5:37
should be however the statement of you Yes Yes, [and] ‘No ‘No;
The however excessive of these from the evil comes.

5:38
You have heard that it was said;
Eye for eye and tooth for tooth.’

5:39
I myself however say to you not to resist the evil [person].
Instead whoever you strike on the right cheek
of you, do turn to him also the other;

5:40
and to the [one] willing you to sue and the tunic of you to take,
do yield to him also the cloak;

5:41
and whoever you will compel to go mile one,
do go with him two.

5:42
To the [one] asking you do give
and the [one] desiring from you to borrow not may you turn away from.

5:43
You have heard that it was said;
You will love the neighbour of you
and will hate the enemy of you.’

5:44
I myself however say to you; do love the enemies of you
do bless those cursing you well do

5:45
so that you may be sons of the Father of you who is in the heavens,
For the sun of Him He makes rise on evil and good
and He sends rain on righteous and unrighteous.

what reward have you?

5:47
