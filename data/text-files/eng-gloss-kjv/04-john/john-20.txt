and to the other disciple whom was loving the Jesus
and she says to them;
They have taken away the Lord out of the tomb,
and not we know where they have laid Him.

one at the head and one at the feet,
where was laying the body the of Jesus.

Woman, why weep you?

Because they have taken away the Lord of mine,
and not I know where they have laid Him.

20:14
and These things having said she turned to the back
and she sees the Jesus already standing [there]
