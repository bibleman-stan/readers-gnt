15:1
I myself am the vine the true,
and the Father of Mine the vinedresser is.

15:2
Every branch in Me myself not bearing fruit
He takes away it,
and every one the fruit bearing
He prunes it
that fruit more it may bear.

15:3
Already you yourselves clean are because of the word
that I have spoken to you.

15:4
do abide in Me myself
and I myself in you.
Even as the branch not is able fruit to bear of itself
only unless it shall abide in the vine,
so neither [are able] you
only unless in Me myself you shall abide.

15:5
I myself am the vine,
you [are] the branches.
The [one] abiding in Me myself and I myself in him, he bears fruit much;
For apart from Me not you are able to do no [thing].

15:6
Only unless someone shall abide in Me myself,
he is thrown out like the branch and is dried up,
and they gather them
and into the fire cast, and it is burned.

15:7
If you shall abide in Me myself
and the declarations of Mine in you shall abide,
whatever if you shall wish
do yourself ask and it will happen to you.

15:8
In this is glorified the Father of Mine,
that fruit much you may bear
and you may become to Me disciples.

15:9
even as has loved Me the Father,
I myself also you loved;
do abide in the love the of Mine.

15:10
If the commandments of Mine you shall keep,
you will abide in the love of Mine;
even as I myself the commandments of the Father of Mine have kept,
and I abide of Him in the love.

15:11
These things I have spoken to you
that the joy the of Mine in you may be
and the joy of you may be full.

15:12
This is the commandment the of Mine
that you may love one another even as I loved you.

15:13
Greater than this love no [one] has
that one the life of him may lay down for the friends of him.

15:14
You yourselves friends of Mine are if you shall do
what things as much as I myself command you.

15:15
no longer I name you servants,
for the servant not knows what is doing his the master;
You however I have called friends,
because all things that I heard from the Father of Mine I have made known to you.

15:16
Not you yourselves Me chose,
but I myself chose you
and appointed you
that you yourselves may go and fruit you may bear,
and the fruit of you may remain;
so that which one maybe you may ask the Father in the name of Me, He may give you.

15:17
These things I command you
that you may love one another.

15:18
If the world you hates,
you know that Me myself before you it has hated.

15:19
If of the world you were,
the world then would the [as its] own was loving [you];
because however of the world not you are,
but I myself chose you out of the world,
on account of this hates you the world.

15:20
do remember the word
that I myself said to you:
Not is a servant greater than the master of him.
If Me myself they persecuted
also you they will persecute;
if the word of Mine they kept
also the yours they will keep.

15:21
But these things all they will do against you on account of the name of Me,
because not they know the [One] having sent Me.

15:22
only unless I come
and I said to them,
sin not they had;
now however excuse not they have for the sin of them.

15:23
The [one] Me myself hating also the Father of Mine hates.

15:24
If the works not I had done among them
that no other did,
sin not they had;
now however both they have seen
and have hated both Me myself and the Father of Mine;

15:25
But [this is] that may be fulfilled the word the in the law of them written
that They hated Me without cause.’

15:26
When now may come the Helper
whom I myself will send to you from the Father,
the Spirit the of truth
who from the Father goes forth,
He will bear witness concerning Me;

15:27
Also you yourselves now bear witness, because from [the] beginning with Me you are.
